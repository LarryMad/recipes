Soothe digestion, heartburn, nausea, and other stomach trouble with the fresh
ginger in this natural remedy smoothie recipe.

Ingredients:

1 banana, sliced
¾ c (6 oz) vanilla yogurt
1 Tbsp honey
½ tsp freshly grated ginger

Directions:

COMBINE the banana, yogurt, honey, and ginger. Blend until smooth.

NUTRITION (per serving):
157 cals
1 g fat
0.8 g sat fat
57 mg sodium
34 g carbs
28 g sugars
1.5 g fiber
5 g protein
