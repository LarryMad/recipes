Busy Day Gingerbread

½ cup Lard or Crisco (I use butter)
½ cup warm water
2 ½ cups plain flour
¾ teaspoon salt
1 cup molasses - if you can get it use unprocessed molasses 
like you might get at aroadside stand or farmers market
1 teaspoon Baking Soda
1 teaspoon Ginger
½ teaspoon cinnamon
¼ teaspoon cloves (or allspice)
¼ teaspoon nutmeg
1 cup nuts (optional)

*	Preheat oven to 350 degrees
*	Melt lard in large sauce pan; remove from heat.
*	Add water and molasses.
*	Sift together remaining ingredients, add to mixture in pan.
*	Beat until well mixed.
*	Pour into greased 9” square pan
*	Bake 25 minutes
