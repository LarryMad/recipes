"A wonderful, rich, anchovy dressing makes this salad a meal. Serve with crusty Italian Bread."

6 cloves garlic, peeled, divided
3/4 cup mayonnaise
5 anchovy fillets, minced
6 tablespons grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1 teaspot Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspot Dijon mustard
1 tablespon of lemon juice, or more to taste
salt to taste 
ground black paper to taste
1/4 cup olive oil
4 cups day-old bread, cubed
1 head romaince lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces
