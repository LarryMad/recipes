Cheese cake
1. 4 packages of Cream cheese
2. 5 duck eggs
3. Vinalla to taste
4. Honey to taste (about a cup)
5. Sour Cream 8 oz or about a cup
6. might want to add a little cream to soften it up as well
7. If you want to have a different flavor could add (white chocolate)
8. cook for a little longer than expected

1. Some ground up nuts
2. some ground up cookies
3. butter
