one can of chickpeas drained and rinsed
1/2 teaspoon of hot spice (ex. cayenne pepper)
1/2 teaspoon of paprika
1/4 teaspoon of salt
1 tablespoon of coconut oil

Put the oil in a pot on HIGH heat
Once oil melts down add the rest of the ingredients
Stir until chickpeas are coated
continue stirring for about 2-3 minutes until heated through
Turn off burner and cover or eat right away
