Material : Long slender white rice-cake/boiled fish paste/cayenne/Korean hot pepper paste/salt/starch syrup/sugar/Tongkkae/welsh onion/onion/carrot/sesame oil

1. Put fittingly and brew water in wide pan.
2. Put Korean hot pepper paste, and boiled fish paste if water boils. 
3. Put and cook rice cake and welsh onion, onion, carrot'.
4. Put cayenne.
5. Exhort and put sugar or starch syrup to salt.
6. If boil all, put Tongkkae or sesame oil.
