Ingredients
-Rice
-Tomatoes
-Pepper
-Onions
-Seasoning Cubes
-Salt
-Soya Oil

Instructions.
1.	Rince rice and add into a pot of boiling water.
2.	Drain rice and set aside after it has boiled for 5-10mins.
3.	Blend tomatoes, pepper and onion into a mix.
4.	Heat little oil and add blended mix.
5.	Allow to heat for a bit and add enough water to cook rice to satisfaction.
6.	Add salt and seasoning to taste.
7.	Bring to a boil and add rice with very little stir.
8.	Allow to heat till dry.
