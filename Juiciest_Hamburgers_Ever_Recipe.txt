- Preheat grill for high heat
- In a large bowl, mix:
	- ground beef
	- egg
	- bread crumbs
	- evaporated milk
	- Worcestershire sauce
	- cayenne pepper
	- garli
using your hands
- Form the mixture into 8 hamburger patties
- Lightly oil the grill grate
- Grill patties 5 minutes per side, or until well done
