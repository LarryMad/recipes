Recipe

1. Beef  2kg
2. Onion 0.5 kg
3. Ginger 100g
4. Cummins 1teaspoon
5. Clove 6pc
6. Big Cardamom 2pc
7. Peanuts 50g
8. Curd 100gm
9. Red chilly powder 2 teaspoon
10. Salt 2teaspoon
11. Mustard oil 100ml
12. Raw papaya 1 pc small
13. Dry dates 4pc

Procedure

1. Slice the meat in thin pieces
2. Wash the meat
3. Add salt and oil to it(completely)
4. Grind all the spices finely (papaya separately)
5. Add all the spices and curd to meat and leave it for 3 hours for marination (do not add papaya now)
6. Add papaya 15 minutes before grilling
7. Put the meat on barbeque.
