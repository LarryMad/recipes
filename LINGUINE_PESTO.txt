INGREDIENTS

Ingredients for 6 people      

1 box Barilla® Pronto® Half-Cut Linguine
3 cups cold water1 jar Barilla® Pesto
½ cup Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese grated
Salt to taste


INSTRUCTIONS

1
Pour the whole box of pasta into a large skillet (approximately 12 inches in diameter).  Pour 3 cups of cold water into the pan ensuring that the water covers the pasta.  

2
Turn on the burner to high and set your timer for 10 minutes.  Optional:  add about 1/2 teaspoon of salt to taste.  

3
Cook on high, stirring regularly, until most of the water is absorbed, about 10 minutes but may vary by stove.  

4
Add pesto and stir to combine.  Remove the skillet from the heat and top with cheese.
