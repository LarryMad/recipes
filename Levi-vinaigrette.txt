Ingredients:
1 Cup Exta Virgin Olive Oil	
1⁄3	Cup balsamic vinegar (or Apple Cider Vinegar)
2 Tblspn Dijon mustard
5 cloves garlic	crushed
1⁄2	Tblspn Italian Seasoning


Directions:
1. Crush cloves of garlic and add them and all other ingredients to a small bowl that has an airtight cover.
2. Mix ingredients, particularly trying to get the mustard unclumped.
3. Cover and shake vigorously to get ingredients thoroughly mixed
4. Put in freezer for 15 minutes to cool and congeal.
5. Remove from freezer and put in fridge (and/or add to salad).
