PB&J 2.0

Ingredients:
4 Tbsp of Smooth Peanut Butter
2 Tbsp of Strawberry Jam
2 Slices of Bread

Instructions:
1. Toast the bread to a golden crisp.
2. Spread 2 tablespoons of smooth peanut butter on one side of each toasted bread.
3. Spread 2 tablespoons of strawberry jame on the peanut butter side of one toasted bread.
4. Place the bread together as to keep the spread in between.
5. Enjoy the noms!
