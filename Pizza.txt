Pizza Recipe

1. First take the pizza dough(200 g) and mead it well.
2. Then spread out the dough into a circle of whatever thicknes you like, i personally roll it into a thin sheet.
3. Now , spread marinara sauce over the uncooked base.
4. Add cheese and the toppings you would like to have, if like pineapple in your pizza please change your tastebuds.
5. Now put the pizza into the oven untill it looks ready to be eaten.
