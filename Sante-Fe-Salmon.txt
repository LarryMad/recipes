Santa Fe Salmon

1lb of salmon, skinned removed
1 cup mayonaise
1 jar salsa
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
juice of one lime
1Tablespoon vermouth


strain salsa. Mix with mayo, garlic, lime juice and vermouth. Marinade salmon fillets in mixture for 15 min. 

Cook in pan on stove for approximately 5 min on a side. Fillets should break apart easily with a fork.
