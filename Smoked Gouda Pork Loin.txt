Ingredients:
1 - 2" Bone In Pork Chop
2 ounces of smoked gouda
1 - teaspoon of olive oil
salt
pepper
parsley

-----------
Mix Parselt, salt, pepper, and olive oil in dish.
Cut pocket in pork chop and stuff gouda rubbed in dressing, then rub pork with dressing on outside.
Use tooth picks to sandwich and grill on 400 degress for 7 min on each side.
