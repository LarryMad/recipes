3 Egg Whites
1 Egg
2 slices of swiss cheese
1/4/ cup milk
olive oil

Directions:
1. Mix 3 Egg Whites, 1 Egg and 1/4 cup of milk in bowl
2. Heat frying pan on medium heat and apply thin film of olive oil
3. Add contents from step 1 to heated frying pan
4. Continue to heat until contents are firm
5. Flip contents in frying pan
6. Add 2 slices fo swiss cheese to one side of omlet and fold over the other side
7. remove from pan and serve.
