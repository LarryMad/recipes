
Ingredients:                                                                                             
- 1 large egg                                                                                        
- Cooked rice                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                   
Directions:                                                                                               
1. Crack an egg and drop it on a single serving cooked rice.                                                 
2. Season it with soy saurce.                                
3. A hint of bonito (or seaweed) dashi is recommended.
