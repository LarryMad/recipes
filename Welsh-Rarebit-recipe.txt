Ingredients:
 - 2 slices of brown or white bread
 - dash of Worcester Sauce
 - Approx 4oz of grated hard cheese - e.g cheddar / red leicester.

Directions:
 1. Pre-heat a grill on a high heat.

 2. Toast one side of the bread until lightly grilled

 3. Remove bread from the grill and sprinkle the cheese generously onto the other side of the bread.

 4. Sprinkle Worcester Sauce over the cheese to taste.
  
 5. Grill bread until cheese is bubbly and starts to turn brown.

 6. Remove from grill and allow to cool slightly.
