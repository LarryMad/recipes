Aalu Paratha:

- Boil the potatoes
- Mesh the potatoes after peeling off them
- Mix some salt, red pepper, butter and black pepper
- Keep that as such for a while
- Get two tortillas
- Heat them a little bit on one side
- Put some butter on that side
- Now put the meshed potatoes on each of them
- Heat the other sides and join the sides with the potato masala

Here you go!
Enjoy :)
