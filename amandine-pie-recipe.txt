Ingredients:
 - 125g powdered almond
 - 125g powdered sugar
 - 3 eggs
 - 75g butter
 - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
 - 1 teaspoon rum
 - 6 syrup pear halves
 - 1 "pate sablée"

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter.
  2. Alternately add powdered sugar and almond.
  3. Stir in vanilla.
  4. cook the pate sablée in the oven for 15 min
  5. get the pate out and place the pear halves on it symetrically
  6. pour in your mix
  7. cook in the oven for 30 min.
