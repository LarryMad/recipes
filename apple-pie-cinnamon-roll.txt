- 5 apples 
- 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon of sugar
- 1/2 lemon
- 2 cans of pillsbury cinnamon rolls
- 1/4 stick butter


1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. Lightly coat bottom of medium sized baking pan with butter.

3. Slice fresh apples. 

4. Squeeze half lemon throughout apples slices and add sugar and cinnamon.

5. Mix contents in large mixing bowl.

6. Lay 1 can of flatened cinammon rolls on base of baking pan to create
   base and side crusts of pie.

7. Pour apples slices in pan

8. Lay 1 can of flatened cinammon rolls ontop of apple slices to create 
   top layer of crust of pie.

9. Cook on coventional oven for 25 min - 30 min or until cinnamon crust 
   has browned.

10. Let sit for 15 min, then add icing

11. Enjoy!
