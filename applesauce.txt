4 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and chopped
3/4 cup water
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger
1/16 teaspoon ground cloves

In a saucepan, combine water and sugar. 
When the sugar has dissolved, add the spices and apples
Stir well to coat the apples in liquid.
Cover, and cook over medium heat for 15 minutes, or until the apples are soft.
Mash with a fork.
Serve hot or chilled.
