STEP 1 Melt the butter, sugar and vanilla in a medium-sized saucepan.
STEP 2 Remove from the heat.
STEP 3 Add mashed bananas and stir through until just combined.
STEP 4 Add egg and mix well. Stir in the flour, then pour in the milk and fold in lightly.
STEP 5 Bake at 170C for approximately 40 minutes.
