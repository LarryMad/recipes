Ingredients:
-2 lbs. tuna steak (very fresh 1 inch thick)
-4 tbsp. olive oil
-2.5 tsp. salt
-0.5 tsp black pepper
-2 limes, zested
-1 tsp. wasabi powder
-6 tbsp. freshly squeezed lime juice (~3 limes)
-2 tsp. soy sauce
-10 dashes of hot sauce (preferably Tabasco)
-1 to 2 ripe Hass avocados (medium dice)
-0.25 cups minced scallions, white and green parts
-0.25 cups red onion, small dice

Directions:
1. Brush tuna steaks with olive oil. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Place tuna steaks in a very hot saute pan and cook for only 1 minute on each side. Set aside on a platter.
3. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, salt, pepper, lime zest, wasabi powder, lime juice, soy sauce, and hot sauce. Add the avocados to the vinaigrette.
4. Cut the tuna in chunks and place in a large bowl. Add the scallions and red onion, mixing well. Pour the vinaigrette mixture over the tuna and carefully mix.
