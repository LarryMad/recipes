BBQ Pork Chop

Preheat oven to 375

Melt butter in a pan on the stove

Add pork chops to pan and cook for approximately 2 minutes on each side or until each side begins to brown

Put pork chops in a shallow baking dish and add to oven Bake approximately 5 minutes

Add BBQ sauce to each side of pork chop and continue cooking for 10-15min until middle is no longer pink
