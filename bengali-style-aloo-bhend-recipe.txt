Ingredients:-
- Kalo Jeera (Black Cummin)
- 3 green chili
- 4 medium sized potato
- 500 gm ladies finger

Directions :-

1. Wash and rinse the vegetables.
2. Cut the potato in medium sized pieces.
3. Cut the ladies finger into equal halves.
4. In a fan put 2 teaspoon oil and heat it.
5. Cut the chilies in the middle and add them to the oil.
6. Add Kalo Jeera now.
7. Wait for it fry a bit.
8. Now add potato and ladies finger and fry till both the vegetable turn little brown.
9. Add water and put a cover for 10 mins.
10. Serve it hot :)
