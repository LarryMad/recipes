Ingredients
- 7 chocolate
- 4 butter
- 7 brown sugar
- 6 flour

Recipe
1. Mix up the flour and the butter with your hands.

2. Pat the mixture into the tray, make it hard, then cook it at a temperature.

3. Heat brown sugar at higher temperature, making it brown and liquidy.

4. Melt chocolate in a bowl over boiling water.

5. Spread caramel (melted, heated sugar) over shortcate (mixed up, cooked, flour/butter mixture) in thick layer.

6. Leave to cool.

7. Spread chocolate over caramel in thinner layer.

8. Cool in fridge/freezer.

9. Eat.
