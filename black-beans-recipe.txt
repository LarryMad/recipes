Ingredients:
 - 1 lb of ground beef
 - 1 box of black beans and rice
 - 1 Tbs of olive oil
 - 1 1/3 cup of water

Directions:
  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Get rid of fat
  3. Add water
  4. Add all ingredients in box
  5. mix until powder is fully dissolved
  6. wait until water boils
  7. low heat
  8. Set timer to 25 min
  9. Let sit for 5 minutes.
