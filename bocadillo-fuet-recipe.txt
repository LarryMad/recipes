Ingredients:
 - 60g fuet
 - 1/3 Mediterranean bread
 - 1 tomato "de colgar"
 - Alberquina olive oil

Directions:
 1. Slice the bread in 2 horizontally.
 
 2. Cut the tomato in 2 and brush it onto the inside sides of the bread slices.
 
 3. Split some oil onto the inside sides of the bread and join the bread again so the oil gets mixed uniformly.

 4. Slice the fuet (1-2mm).
 
 5. Spread the fuet slices all over the one inside slice of bread uniformly and then put the other inside slice of bread over it.
 
 6. Yum.
