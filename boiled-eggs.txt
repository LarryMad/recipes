Ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- 700 ml H20

Directions:
1. Put water into pot
2. Put pot on stove
3. Turn heat to high
4. Once water begins to boil, add eggs to water
5. Cook eggs for 10 minutes
6. Turn off stove
7. Pour water from pot into sink
8. Add cool water into pot
9. Peel shells from eggs
10. Rinse remaining eggshells away
11. Eat
