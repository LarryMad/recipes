Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 2 glasses of water

Directions:
 1. Boil the water in a can

 2. Put the eggs when the water starts to boil

 3. Take the eggs off after 5 minutes if you like the eggs soft. Seven minutes if you like the eggs more cooked.


 Tip: Put one spoon of vinager in the water, before the eggs, to easy take off the egg shell.
