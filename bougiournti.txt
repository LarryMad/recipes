Ingredients:
  -  200 gr greek feta cheese
  -  100 gr grated yellow cheese
  -  2 tomatos, cut in cubes
  -  1 horned hot pepper, cut in slices
  -  chilli 
  -  1 soup spoon oregano grated
  -  30 ml olive oil

Directions:

 - Preheat oven at about 200 degrees
 
 - Put in an oven dish the feta cheese and on top of it the tomatos, the sliced pepper and the chilli.
   all over the previous you spread the grated cheese and leave it in the oven for about 15 minutes.
   
 - When the cheese has melted you take the oven dish out and put the olive oil and the oregano 
   and finally serve.
