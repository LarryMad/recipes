Ingredients:
-6 cups of water
-2 dry ramen packets
-two handfuls of spinach
-two eggs
-chopped celery and onions
-1 bullion cube
-pink sea salt
-paprika
-black pepper

Sautee' chopped celery and onions
Bring six cups of water to a boil
Place 2 dry ramen packets into boiling water
Crack and stir the eggs
Place eggs into boiling water with ramen
Wait 2 minutes
Place spinach, sauteed celery, and onions into water
Pour about 5 cups of the water off of the mix
Season to perfection!
