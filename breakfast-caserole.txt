Ingredients:

8 eggs
6 pieces of bread
16oz pork sausage
8oz graded cheese of choice

- preheat oven to 350
- cook sausage and crumble
- mix eggs in a bowl by hand or with a mixer
- lightly oil a 9x13 pan
- pour eggs in pan
- evenly distribute sausage in pan
- tear up bread into small pieces and push into egg solution
- evenly distribute cheese in pan and push into egg solution
- cook 20 to 25 minutes, or until egg is cooked (not overly done)
- cut into squares and serve
