How to make a brownie?
Pre-heat oven to 350F
Add 2 eggs, 1/4 cup water, 1/2 cup vegetable oil and cake mix powder to a bowl and beat until the contents mix well.
Apply oil to the bottom of a 9*13 pan and pour the mixture into the pan.
Bake in oven for 28-30 minutes. Do the tooth pick test to check if the cake is done.
