Buffalo Chicken Meatloaf

2lbs ground chicken (I like to grind it myself using boneless, skinless chicken breasts in a blender)
1 cup hot sauce
1 or 2 jalapeno peppers
1 cup bread crumbs
2 eggs
1 onion

Ranch or Blue cheese dressing

Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Mix hot sauce and ground chicken in bowl, refigerate for at least 4 hours
Dice peppers and onions
Add all ingredients except dressing in a large bowl and mix thorougly
Press mixture into loaf pan and put in preheated oven
Bake for 50 minutes
Remove pan and drain excess liquid
Top with dressing and bake for another 10 minutes.
