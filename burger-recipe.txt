Vegan Burger.

Start with the vegan pattie. Farmfresh does a good one. Available in Vons or many other supermarkets

Bread roll of your choice but i like ciabatta.

slice open the roll and remove any excess dough.
add;
sliced avocado
a slice of sweet white onion
a few baby tomatoes
a lttle garlic salt (optional)

Place the cooked patties on the bun, close and eat. YUM !
