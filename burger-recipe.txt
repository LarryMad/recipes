Awesome Burger Recipe

Ingredients:
-1/3lb organig grass fed ground beef
-cheddar cheese
-pepper jack cheese
-fried onion rings
-sliced organic brown tomatoe
-butter lettuce
-bread & butter pickles
-organin mayo
-hawaiian burger buns

Instructions:
-Cook the burger in a teaspoon of butter, evenly on both sides.
-Spread mayo on both halves of the burger bun and put on the skillet you cooked the burger on, mayo side down, until they turn golden brown.
-build as such:
	bottom bun
	pickles
	burger
	cheeses
	tomato
	lettuce
	fried onion ring
	top bun

	*add your favorite condiments. mine is Frenchs Bbq tangy mustard!
