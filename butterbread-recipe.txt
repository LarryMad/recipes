Ingredients:
 - 1 slice of bread
 - 50 gram of butter
 - 1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon of sea-salt (optional)

Directions:
 1. Put the butter on top of the slice of bread
 
 2. Gently spread the butter on the upper side of the slice of bread using a knife.
    Caution! Knifes are dangerous! Don't hurt yourself!
 
 3. (optional) If you are a real gourmet and like to take the time to create a
    real masterpiece of haute cuisine you may know apply a 1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon
    of sea-salt.

 4. Et voilà ! Bon appetit !
    ( planking is important in french cuisine recipes )
