Ingredients:
Spaguetti
Cream
Bacon
Onion
Olive oil
Garlic
Salt
Pepper

Directions
- Put spaguetti in boiled water for about 10 min
- Simoultanesly put in a grill, with olive oil, onion and garlic. Add salt and pepper.
- When the onion is soft enough, add the bacon.
- After 10 min, put spaguetti in the grill.
- Add cream
- Enjoy! ; )
