Ingredients:
 - Milk
 - 'Cornflakes' Cereal (Rooster Cereal)
 - Sugar
 - Oats
 - 1 Banana
 - 1 Cereal Boal
 
Directions:
 1. Pour cereal flakes into cereal bowl.
 
 2. Pour the amount of sugar you desire
    on top of cereal flakes in bowl.
 
 2. Pour the amount of oats you desire
    on top of sugar in bowl.
	
 3. Cut the banana into cylinder pieces
    and throw those pieces into the bowl too.
	
 4. Pour milk. :D
