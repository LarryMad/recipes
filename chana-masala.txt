1 tin chickpeas
2 tomatoes
1 onion
1 inch ginger
1 cup water
1 tsp cumin seeds
pinch asfoetida
1 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp chilli powder
1 tsp garam masala

1. Heat oil in a pot.
2. Add cumin seeds and asafoetida.
3. Add onion and cook until browned.
4. Add tomatoes, turmeric and chilli powder. Cook until tomatoes start
   to break down.
5. Add water, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Add garam masala and serve.
