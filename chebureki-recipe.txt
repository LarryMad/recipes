Chebureki are half moon-shaped dough filled with meat and fried in oil. The dough is soft and crunchy with juicy meat inside.

  Ingredients for dough:
3.5 oz warmed milk
3 eggs
2 tbs oil
2 tbs vodka
1 tsp salt
3 cups flour - See more at: http://leascooking.blogspot.fr/2012/08/traditional-russian-chebureki.html#sthash.1rz9uKij.dpuf
