Ingredients:
 - Grated cheese (any strength)
 - Wholemeal bread
 - Mustard

Directions:
 1. Toast one side of the bread under a grill.
 2. Spread a thin layer of mustard onto the non-toasted side of bread.
 3. Sprinkle generous amounts of grated cheese on top of the mustard.
 4. Place the toast back under the grill, with the cheese facing upwards.
 5. Toast until golden brown.
 6. Enjoy!
