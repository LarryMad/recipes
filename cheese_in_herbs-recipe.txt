Ingredients:
  - 500 g of chicken breast
  - 200 g of cream cheese
  - 100 ml of milk
  - 1 teaspoon of basil
  - 1 teaspoon of provance herbs
  - 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  - 200 g of spaghetti
  - salt

Directions:
 1. Cut the chicken breast into ~2 cm cubes, heat olive oil on the frying pan and fry the chicken
 2. When it is ready, put the chicken on a plate and add milk and cream chease to the frying pan; mix untill cream cheese fully melts
 3. Mix the sauce with fried chicken breast, add basil and herbs; if not salty enough add some salt
 4. In the meantime boil spaghetti in salted water
 5. Serve hot sauce on spaghetti, you can decorate it with fresh basil leave
