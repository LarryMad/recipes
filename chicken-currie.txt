Ingredients:
 - 5 chicken pieces
 - garam masala
 - one medium onion
 - 1/2 garlic
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 cup mustard oil
 - 2 cup boiling water
 - 2 potatoes

Directions:
 1. heat oil and put cut onions and fry till golden brown
 
 2. put chicken pieces, potato pieces, salt, garlic and fry for 10 minutes
 
 3. add water and garam masala, mix, and leave for 15 minutes
