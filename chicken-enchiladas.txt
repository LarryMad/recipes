Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds chicken breast or chicken legs
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 1 onion chopped
 - One 8-ounce can green tomatillo salsa
 - 1 tablespoons parsley
 - 1/8 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - cheddar chesse shredded
 - cream to own taste
 - 1/8 gallon half and half milk

Directions:
 1. Cook chicken on a boiling pot for 30 to 40 minutes until tender
 2. Golden brown onions in a large pan.
 3. Add chopped garlic cloves, the green tomatillo salsa, parsley, cumin, salt, and shredded chicken.
 4. Stir, over low heat for 5 minutes.
 5. Warm up tortilla on a flat pan.
 6. Rolled the tortilla with the chicken
 6. Add cheese, cream and half anf half milk and salsa on top of enchilas
 7  Bake for 15 minutes for 10 or 12 minutes.

 Enjoy!
