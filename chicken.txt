1. Cut chicken breast in half
2. Set pan over high flame for 1 min
3. add oil to coat pan
4. reduce pan to 4
5. add chicken
6 wait one minute
7. flip chicken over
8. put lid on
9. reduce heat to low flame
10. set timer for 10 min
11. turn heat off after 10 min
12. let sit with pan lid on for 10 min
13. you're done!
