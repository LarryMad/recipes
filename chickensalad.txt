Chicken Salad

Ingredients:
* 1 or 2 cans of chicken
* Mayo to taste
* 2-4 stalks of celery chopped finely
* 1 hot house cucumber unpeeled and diced or 1 regular cucumber peeled and diced
* 1 small green apple diced (peeled if you like)
* Optional: handful of chopped walnuts

Directions
* Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
* Serve!
