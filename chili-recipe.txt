Ingredients:
 - 4 pounds ground chicken
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 6 tablespoons ground cumin
 - 2 tablespoons cumin seeds
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 1/4 cup corn flour
 - 1 cup flax seeds
 - One 15-ounce can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
 - One 15-ounce can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
 - Three 15-ounce cans black beans, drained and rinsed
 - 4 lbs Hanover tomatoes

Directions:
 1. Brown the chicken in a large bot and drain off the excess.
 2. Add the tomatos, chili powder, cumin, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 4. Mix the corn flour, flax seeds and 1/2 cup water in a small bowl.  Add to chili.
 5. Add the beans and simmer for 10 more minutes.
