Ingredients:
 - 2 cup of pumpkin shreds
 - 3 cups all-purpose flour
 - 2 teaspoons salt
 - 2 eggs
 - 2 cup water

Directions:
 1. In a large bowl, combine the pumpkin, flour, salt, and water. Put eggs
    into another bowl and stir well. Mix until smooth.  

 2. Add a teaspoon of oil, using low heat. Add 3/4 cup of batter once a time
 
 3. Fry 1 minute until the batter doesn't stick to the pan, turn over and fry
    for another 1 minute. Flip sides once a while. Cool completely.


# for 6-8 pancakes
