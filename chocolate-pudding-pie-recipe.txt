Chocolate Pudding Pie Recipe

1 premade gluten-free graham cracker crust
2 boxes of My-T-Fine Chocolate pudding mix
4 cups milk

Combine pudding mix and milk in a saucepan on medium heat. Use a wisk to stir until the pudding starts to bubble and get thick.

Pour into premade pie crust

Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour

Serve with whipped cream
