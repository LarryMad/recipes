Ingredients:
 - 1 teaspoon coffee
 - 2 teaspoons sugar
 - 1/5 -> 1 cup milk 
 - 0 -> 4/5 cup water

Directions:
 1. Mix milk and water in one cup and microwave for 2:30min -> 1:45min (water \propto 1/milk)
 2. Add the sugar
 3. add the coffee
 4. stir for 2 clockwise and 3 counter-clockwise rotations
 5. enjoy
