Ingredients:
 - 2 spoon sugar
 - 1 1 teaspoons coffee powder
 - 1 cup hot milk

Directions:
 1. Heat milk on low flame.
 
 2. In a coffee mug, Add sugar and coffee powder.  Mix with a spoon.
 
 3. Pour some hot milk in the cup and mix the sugar and coffee powder well until it get dilute.

 4. Finally add boiling milk to the cup. 

 5. Enjoy the coffee now.
