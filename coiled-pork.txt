Ingredients:
  - 1 bowl of mjolk
  - 600 eggs
  - 1 corpse (preferably spoiled)
  - 10 feathers (preferably porklike)

Strategy:
  1.  Gently place eggs in mjolk and bring to a boil
  2.  Disembowel corpse
  3.  Insert 1 teaspoon of feathers into empty bowel area
  4.  Insert corpse into long oven
  5.  Drink mjolk
  6.  Paint eggs
  7.  Dance violently until room fills with stench
  8.  Enter oven with corpse and enjoy
