Ingredients:
 - 1/3 cup sugar
 - 1/2 cup brown sugar
 - 1 cup all-purpose flour
 - 3 tablespoons cocoa
 - 1 teaspoon baking soda
 - 1.5 oz melted dark chocolate
 - 9 oz chopped dark chocolate
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 egg
 - 1 stick of butter
 - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract


Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
 
 2. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt. 
 
 3. In a large bowl, cream butter, sugar and brown sugar. Add eggs and vanilla.
    Mix until smooth.  Stir in melted dark chocolate.

 4. Combine dry ingredients with wet ingredients in three additions, being
    careful not to over mix. Stir in chopped dark chocolate.	
 
 5. Roll the cookie dough into 1-inch balls and place on a cookie sheet, being
    careful not to over crowd.
	
 6. Bake for 12-14 minutes at 350 degrees F.
