cookies-recipe

How to make cookies, in any amount you like.

Ingredients		amount in relative weight
Butter			1/4
sugar			1/4
flower			97/200
lemon peel		1/100
salt			1/200

Pre heat over at 375 fahrentheit or 190 centigrade.
Take a stick of butter and weigh it.
Add the other ingredients to get to the ratio discribed above.
knead them together.
Don't eat all the dough.
schape the roll into a cilinder.
cut slices about a dime thick from the dough.
Put a sheet of baking paper on a tray.
Put the slices of dough on top of it.
Bake for about 15 minutes.
check to see if they're done.
