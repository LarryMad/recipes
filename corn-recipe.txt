Ingredients:
 - 1 Corn on the Cob
 - 3 cups of water
 - Pinch of salt
 - 1/2 teaspoon of butter

Directions:
  1. Put water in cooking pot. Put corn into pot.  Add salt.  Bring water to boil.
  2. Boil corn for 20 minutes, or until it meets desired tenderness.
  3. Drain water from pot.  Rub butter on corn.
