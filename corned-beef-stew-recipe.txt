Ingredients:
 - 2 cups pepper blend
 - 6 oz corned beef (half of a can)
 - 1 tablespoon dried thyme
 - 1 teaspoons curry powder
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon black pepper
 - 3 eggs 
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1/4 cup vegetable or broth
 - 1 tablespoon tomato paste

Directions:
 1. Heat the oil in a small pot over medium high heat for 2 minutes.

 2. Add the pepper blend, corned beef, spices, and tomate paste into the
    pot. Cook for 12 minutes, then add the whisked eggs into the pot.

 3. Stir around with spoon and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
