ous Cous Pasta Bake
You will need:

    300g of cornflour
    2oz of muesli
    60g of cous cous
    355g of pasta
    187g of grated cheese

Instructions:

    Pre-heat the oven to 210C
    Deseed the cornflour
    Season the muesli
    Deseed the cous cous
    Crunch the cous cous
    Chop the cornflour
    Dice the cous cous
    Knead the muesli
    Slice the cous cous
    Stir in the pasta and pour mixture into oven dish
    Top with the grated cheese
    Bake for 34 minutes
