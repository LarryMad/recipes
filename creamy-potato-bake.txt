Ingredients
    4 potato large sliced
    3 spring onion chopped
    3 bacon rashers chopped
    300 mls cream
    1 cup cheddar cheese grated
    1 pinch garlic powder *to taste
    45 g cream of chicken instant soup

Method

    STEP 1 Preheat oven to 180C.
    STEP 2 Sauté chopped bacon and set aside.
    STEP 3 Place a layer of potatoes into a casserole dish, sprinkle with garlic powder, bacon, and spring onions.
    STEP 4 Repeat layers 2 or 3 times then sprinkle cheese over top.
    STEP 5 Combine soup with cream and pour over potatoes.
    STEP 6 Bake for 40-60 minutes.
