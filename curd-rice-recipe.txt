Curd Rice Recipe !

Ingredients
- 1 Indian short grain white rice(sona masoori /ponni)
- 1 cup luke warm milk
- 0.5 cup curd/Indian yogurt (at room temperature) 
- 3 Green chillies, finely chopped
- 3 Tablespoon finely chopper coriander leaves
- 1 inch ginger grated
-To taste Salt

For the tempering:
- 1 Tablespoon Oil
- 1 Teaspoon Black Mustard seeds
- 0.5 Tablespoon Urad dal(the white skinned version)
- 1 Dried red chilli 
- 5-6 Curry leaves
- a pinch of Asafoetida

Directions
1. Cook 1 cup rice with 3 cups water (until rice is mushy and all the water absorbed)
2. Cool the rice until it is luke warm 
3. Mash the rice (consistency of thick mashed potatoes)
4. Pour the luke warm milk over the rice and give it a good mix.
5. Pour the curd/Indian yogurt at room temperature and give it a good mix. 
6. With the milk and yogurt added it should reach the consistency of a thin/thick rice pudding. 
	Add luke warm milk or some yoghurt diluted in water to adjust consistency
7. Gently stir in some salt , finely chopped coriander leaves , grater ginger and finely chopped green chillies .

Prepare tempering:
1. In a small pan(ideally tiny iron pan- like the lodge one egg wonder tiny skillet :P) heat oil and add the mustard seeds.
2. When the mustard begins to crackle, add Urad Dal and saute for 10 seconds
3. Add curry leaves, red chilly and asafoetida and saute for 10 more seconds (The curry leaves or urad dal should not turn black)
4. Pour tempering on the rice and give it a gentle mix
5. Serve at room temperature (The perparation could be luke warm , no worries !)
   Do not refrigerate the preparation. DO NOT HEAT it either. 
6. Slurp down this cosmic combination of milk, yoghurt and rice !!! SLURP ! SLURP !
