1. Boil rice in pressure cooker for 20 minutes or upto four whistles.
2. Once pressure is down, open the cooker and allow the rice to cool down for sometime.
3. Add curd/yoghurt freshly made on the rice.
4. Take a pan, heat oil, add mustard, chillies and urad dal in it.
5. Turn off the gas once the urad dal turns red.
6. Add this to the mixture of rice and curd.
7. Use coriander or curry leaves for garnish and serve it fresh.
