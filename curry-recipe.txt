Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds cubed chicken
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 1 yellow onion diced large
 - 2 red bell peppers diced large
 - 2 Tbsp. Tomato paste
 - 4 oz. Green curry paste
 - 3 Tbsp. spicy yellow curry powder
 - 3 cups chicken stock
 - 1 15 oz can coconut milk
 - 2 Tbsp. olive oil

Directions:
 1. Sautee onion, papper, and garlic in olive oil until onion is softened but still crisp
 2. Add the tomato paste, curry paste, and curry powder and allow to toast a bit
 3. Add chicken and sautee until lightly seared on all sides
 4. Add chicken broth and bring to boil
 5. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until broth in pan reduces by at least half
 6. Reduce heat to medium low and slowly stir in coconut milk until fully incorporated
 7. Simmer on medium low, reducing heat as needed to prevent separation until flavor develops fully
