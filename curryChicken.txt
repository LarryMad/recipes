Curry Chicken Recipe:
	1/2 Chicken
	1 cup of yellow curry paste
	2 teaspoon chicken broth
	1 teaspoon sugar
	1 cup soy sauce
	1 cup coconut milk
	2 teaspoon salt

Cooking:
	Heat a wok on high heat for about 30 seconds
	pour in oil
	Stir fry the pre-marianted curry chicken in the wok
	Cook about 10 minutes
	Ready to serve.
