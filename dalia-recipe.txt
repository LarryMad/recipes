Recipe for making tasty dalia

Ingredients required
dalia = 50 g
dal = 20 g
ghee = 10 g
salt = 1/2 teaspoon
water = 300 mL

Process
Roast dalia in a pan with ghee till it turns golden brown.
Add dal, water and salt to it. Close the lid and let it
cook for 20 min at low flame. Close the burner and open the
lid after another 20 min. Mix the preparation well and serve.
