Ingredients

- 1 cup boiling hot lava
- 2 pinches basalt ash
- 600 charred corpses
- 1/2 lb. butter

Preparation

Pour the lava onto your own torso and proceed to writhe in agony, foolish mortal. Mollify the searing pain with a sprinkle of ash. Lay down next to the charred corpses and wait for sweet death to envelop you. Serve with buter.
