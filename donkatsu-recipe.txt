Ingredients:
 - boneless pork chop
 - flour
 - 1 egg
 - panko
 - salt and pepper to season the pork


Directions:
 1. Make cuts on the surface of the pork chop, and season it with salt and pepper.

 2. Coat the seasoned pork with flour by rolling the pork on it.

 3. Dip the pork into mixed egg and coat it thoroughly

 4. Roll the pork into pankos and coat the entire suface of the pork

 5. Deep fri the coated pork until the meat is cooked
