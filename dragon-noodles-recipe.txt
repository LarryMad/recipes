Ingredients:
  Brown sugar
  Siracha
  Eggs
  Noodles
  Soy Sauce
  Green Onion
  Red pepper

  Directions:
  1. Figure out the measurements for the ingredients
  2. Boil some water
  3. Warm up a skillet
  4. Put the noodles in the water
  5. Cook some scrambled eggs on that skillet
  6. Combine the brown sugar siracha and soy sauce into a better sauce, like
  Voltron or the Wu-Tang Clan.
  7. Put the noodles on the skillet
  8. Sprinkle some red pepper
  9. Take out of the skillet and garnish with green onion
