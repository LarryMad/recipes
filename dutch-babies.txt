Recipe for Dutch-babies

1 cup flour
1 cup milk
4 eggs
1 Tsp sugar
1 Tsp vanilla
4 Tbl butter

Place 2 Tbl butter into each of two glass pie dishes
Place pie dishes in oven at 425 deg

Mix up remaining ingredients.
Pour evenly into the pie plates

Cook for 18 minutes.

Serve with maple syrup and fresh fruit.
