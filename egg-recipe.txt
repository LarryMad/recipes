Egg Recipe:
	1. 4 Eggs
		a. Use only two of the yolks
	2. 2 Slices of 2% cheese
	3. 3 Slices Canadian bacon

Preparation:
	-Cook the canadian bacon first.
	-Brown it on both sides, flipping several times.
	-Remove the bacon from the pan.
	-Cook the eggs by themselves into a flat disc.
	-Flip the egg disc once it's stable enough.
	-When it's cooked, remove the disc.
	-Cut the disc in half, spread the cheese evenly on either half.
	-Cut the canadian bacon into small pieces and spread evenly over the cheese.
	-Fold the pieces of egg disc on top of each other with the cheese sides facing each other
	-Eat the meal.
