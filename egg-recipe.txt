Ingredients:
 - 1 egg
 - bowl for boiling
 - water
 - cooker

Directions:
 1. Put egg in bowl.
 
 2. Fill with water till egg is covered
 
 3. Set on cooker and heat till boiling

 4. Boil for 4 minutes

 5. Remove from cooker, discard hot water and cool in tap water

 6. Crack narrow part of shell and peel top half

 7. Sprinkle salt and eat with spoonOA
