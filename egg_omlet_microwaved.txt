Reciepe:: Prepare an egg omlet in microwave oven 

Ingredients:
1 Fresh egg
1 tea spoon of veg. oil or butter
1 microwave safe dish
hint of salt

Procedure:
1. Oil/ butter the dish
2. crack the egg on the dish and poke the yolk
3. add hint of salt and or pepper to taste
4. microwave covered in medium to high power for 60 seconds. check the omlet
   cook for additional 15 to 30 seconds, to harden the Omlet
   
CAution: the dish may get very hot, handle with care
