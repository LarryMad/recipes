Ingredients
- 1 cup elbow macaroni pasta
- 0.5 cup cheese
- salt and pepper to taste
Method
1) Cook 1 cup elbow macaroni pasta al dente . Drain and transfer to baking tray
2) Add some cheese on top of the cooked pasta
3) Season with salt and pepper
4) Bake at 350F for 30 minutes
5) Bring to room temperature and EAT . Lol !
