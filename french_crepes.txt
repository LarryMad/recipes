ingredients :
    - One glass of sugar
    - 4 eggs
    - 150 gr half-salt butter
    - one bottle of bier (25cl)
    - 500 gr of wheat flour
    - milk at your convenience

recipe :
    - merge eggs with sugar
    - add melted butter
    - add bier
    - slowly add flour
    - finish with slowly adding milk
        (I used to finish adding milk when I reach 2 litter of pulp)

cook it, it's done.

nb : you can add orange blossom juice that's better :D
