French Onion Soup

6 Onions
1tbsp olive oil
1tbsp butter
1tsp sugar
1/4tsp salt
1/2 cup wine
3 cups of beef broth
guyere cheese 200g
day old bread

French chop all onions and reduce in the pot on medium heat with the butter olive oil sugar and salt. Once the onions are thoroughly browned deglaze with red wine. Cook off the alcohol on medium high. Add the beef broth and reduce the water level by a quarter. Slice the day old bread into croutons. Grate the gruyerre cheese. Assemble into an over proof bowl the soup first. Second the croutons. Finally sprinkle the grated gruyerre onto the croutons. Broil in the oven on High until cheese is melted. Serve.
