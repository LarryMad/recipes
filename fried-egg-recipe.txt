Ingredients:
 - 1 egg
 - oid (for cooking)
 - salt
 - petter

Directions:
  1. add the oil to the pan and make sure to get it hot.
  2. crack the eggs into the hot oil.
  3. spoon some of the hot oil over the egg (but not the yolk) to make sure that the whites are fully cooked.
  4. put the egg on a plate and season with salt and pepper.
