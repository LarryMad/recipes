Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 onion
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:
 1. Break the eggs into a bowl and whisk them 
 2. Get the oil hot in a pan then fry the sliced onions, 
 then once the onions are brown, pop in the eggs
 3. Wait half a minute then sprinkle the pepper
 4. Turn the egg after like 15 seconds, a few times till it's done.
 5. Serve with bread and baked beans (haricots blanc a la sauce tomate avec
 du pain)
