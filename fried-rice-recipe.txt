Ingredients:
 - 1 cup rice
 - 1 cup peas
 - 1 cup carrots
 - pinch of salt
 - oil

Directions:
  1. In a ricecooker, cook the rice.
  2. Heat a pan, heat a bit of oil on medium heat.
  3. Cook the carrots and peas together until soft.
  4. Add rice and together with a pincb of salt. Stir until fully mixed together.
