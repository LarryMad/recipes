Ingredients:
 - 3 bowls of rice cooked overnight, usually with rice cooker
 - 4 eggs
 - 1 spring onion
 - some salt and pepper
 - cooking oil
 

Directions:
  1. In a large bowl, beat the 4 eggs.
  2. Cut spring onion and the meat small enough.
  3. Soak the beaten egg with the rice and gently separate the rice.
  4. Heat the wok and pour enough oil.  If too little oil the wok may stick.
  5. When oil is heated put spring onion in.
  6. Put meat in.
  7. When meat is done, put the rice in.
  8. At this point fry very quick until the rice is separated completely from each other.
