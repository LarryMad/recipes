4 tomatos (shaved the skin, and cut into pieces)
1/2 onion (sliced into pieces)
4 eggs (completely stirred)
one teaspoon of soybean ketchup (can replace with 1/2 of salt; mix into the stirred eggs)
1/2 teaspoon of pepper (mix into the stirred eggs)
300 gram of butter

Warm up to pan, melt half of the butter.
Fried 1/2 of those stirred eggs, make scrambled eggs.
Use another pan, repeat step one. 
Fried the onion, and when it soften, add the tomatos.
Add the scrambled eggs when you see water coming out from the tomatos. 
Add the other half of the eggs and mix with those in the pan before shut off the oven.
