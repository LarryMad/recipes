Ingredients:
  - some eggs
  - onions
  - spice

Directions:
  1. load up a frying pan with oil and heat until the oil boils.

  2. Break the eggs and put them into a bowl.

  3. Cut up the onions and mix it with the spice.

  4. Load up the frying pan with the mixture of onions and spice

  5. Load up the pan with the eggs in the bowl.

  6. Stir until it looks edible :D.

  7. Done!
