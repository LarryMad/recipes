Ingredients:
 - 2 cups of cut fruits which includes dryfruits, papaya, cherries, apple and bananas
 - 5 spoons of Custard powder
 - 1 cup of milk


Directions:
 1. Cut the Fruits into appropriate shapes and mix them in a large bowl
 2. Add Custard powder and milk to the mixture
 3. Keep the mixture in fridge for about 1 hour for your Custard fruit salad to be ready.
