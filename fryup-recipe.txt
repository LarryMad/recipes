Ingredients:
- 1 Potato
- 1/8 Onion
- 2 Eggs
- (optional) 1/3 Bell Pepper
- (optional) 1 clove garlic
- Salsa and/or Ketchup

Directions:
1. Dice potato and add to frying pan.
2. Dice Onion and add to frying pan.
3. Add any optional ingredients (bell peppers, garlic)
4. Saute.
5. Add eggs and scramble
6. Remove from pan and Top with Salsa or Ketchup
