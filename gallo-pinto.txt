Ingredients:

1 cup of cooked rice
1 cup of cooked beans
2 dices of garlic
1 red sweet pepper
1/2 onion
1 cilantro roll
2 spoon of Salsa Lizano Costa Rican sauce

Preparation:

Chop all the onion, the sweet pepper and garlic, and use a pan and put in with olive oil and mixed it up.
Add the beans to boil, after that add the rice, mixed it up, add the Lizano sauce and the cilantro
