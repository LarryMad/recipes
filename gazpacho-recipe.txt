Ingredients (4 people):
 - 1 kg ripe tomatoes
 - 1/2 a clove garlic
 - 3 spoonfuls olive oil
 - 1 small slice of bread
 - 1 teaspoonful salt
 - 1 spoonful vinager
 - 1 l water (or to the desired consistency)
 
Directions:
  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender and mix. Don't use all the water, you can add more later if you wish.
  2. Strain if necessary.
  3. Serve cold, not chilled.

 You drink it from a glass if you make it thinner or have it as a soup. In the second case you can garnish it with small pieces of tomato, cucumber, bread, green pepper and/or onion.
