Ingredients:
 - giloy branches and leaves
 - tulsi leaves
 - pattharchata leaves

Directions:
 1. Carefully inspect the leaves, sometimes you may find spider web, small insects sticking to them.

 2. Clean all the leaves with drinking water.

 3. Put all the leaves in juicer/mixer. Mix them for a while. Keep adding drinking water periodically. Do not add too much water than necessary. As soon as all the contents are crushed well stop adding water. Let it mix for a while so that everything is fine and smooth.
 
 4. Rinse the juice with a filter. You may want to add little water and mix it and filter it again to extract every drop of the juice. It is ready to drink.
