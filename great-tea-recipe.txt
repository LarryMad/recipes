The great recipe for great tea.
Ingredients:
Pure water
Original chinese red or black tea in bags.
Sugar.
Lemon.
Two porcelain chinese cups with lid, on the small plates and with a silver teaspoon each.
Procedure: 
Boil 450 ml of pure water.
Put a tea bag into each cup.
Pour hot water into the cup.
Cover the cups these lids.
Wait 3 minutes.
Then put one teaspoon of sugar or two into each cup.
Or three to four if you put a slice of lemon too.
