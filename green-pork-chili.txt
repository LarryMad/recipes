Green Pork Chili

Makes 6 servings

INGREDIENTS:
2 pounds cubed boneless pork
1 cup water
2 tablespoons cumin
1 teaspoon chili powder (more or less to taste)
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups slices onions
2 (7-oz.) cans diced green chilies
3/4 cup roasted red peppers, drained and cut into strips
1 (16-oz.) can black beans, drained

Place pork cubes into slow cooker.

Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl, then pour over pork; stir to
combine.

Cover and cook on low for 5 to 7 hours, until pork is cooked through
and tender.
