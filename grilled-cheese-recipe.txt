Ingredients:
- 100g of Cheese
- 2 pieces of bread
- 1 tsp cooking oil

1. Grate the cheese into pieces
2. Slightly heat the pan
3. Pour the cooking oil with the desired amount on the pan
4. Slice the breads into triangular shaped, half pieces.
5. Put the grated cheese on the sliced breads (bottom piece)
6. Cover it with the other sliced breads (top piece)
7. Put them on the pan and wait for few minutes, then flip the breads over.
8. Cheese should start to melt afterwards, put them on the serving plate.
9. Your food is ready to serve, bon appetite!
