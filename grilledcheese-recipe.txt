What you need:
	-sliced bread
	-butter
	-kraft single slices
	-frying pan

directions:
	-butter 2 slices of bread on one side

	-place a single slice of cheese between the buttered
	slices of bread(buttered sides will be on the outside)

	-place the cheese sandwich onto the frying pan and turn
	the heating element to 4.

	-check the bottom of the sandwich periodically

	-once the bottom reaches a nice toasty brown colour
	flip it over to grill the otherside

	-(Note) the other side will fry faster so be careful.

	-place grilled cheese sandwich on plate with ketchup
	on the side and serve.
