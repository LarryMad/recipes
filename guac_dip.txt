Recipe : Guanamole Dip
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Time : 5 minutes
Ingrediants:
1 Avacado
5 Ounces greek yogurt
4 Tbsp Medium Salsa

Directions
1. Rinse avacado
2. Cut avacado in half, separate, remove pit
3. Scoop out flesh of avacado into a bowl
4. Mix avacado, yogurt, and salsa in the bowl with a fork
5. Chill and serve with vegetables
