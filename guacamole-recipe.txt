Ingredients:
 - 2 avocados
 - 1 lime
 - 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
 - salt
 - pepper
 

Directions:
 1. Cut the avocados in half, peel it and remove the core and put it in a bowl
 2. Mush the avocados with a fork
 3. Add the juice of one lime, the teaspoon of chilli flakes 
 4. Mix and season with salt and pepper to your liking
