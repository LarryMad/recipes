Guacamole Recipe

This classic guacamole will be your favorite.

Makes 2 cups, serves 4

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes
Re-Calculate: Ingredients
Serves:  8


Ingredients
4 large (about 8 ounces) ripe Fresh Hass avocados, peeled and pitted
2 Tbsp. fresh lime juice
2 small tomato, seeded, chopped, and well drained
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
2 Serrano chile, chopped
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tsp. fresh or 1/4 teaspoon dried chopped oregano
tortilla chips


Instructions:
Cut avocados into 1/2 inch cubes.

In a medium bowl, combine avocados and lime juice.

Fold in tomato, green onion, cilantro, chile, oregano, and salt.

To store, place a piece of plastic wrap directly on the surface of the Guacamole and refrigerate.

Serve with tortilla chips.
