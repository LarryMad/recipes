Ingredients:
 - 1 pack of gulab jamun mix
 - 4 cups water
 - 3 cups sugar
 - Oil for deep fry

Directions:
  1. Add a cup of water to the gulab jamun mix and make a dough.
  2. Make balls out of this batter and let them cool for 5 minutes in the freezer.
  3. Simultaneously add 3 cups of water to 3 cups of sugar and heat it until all the sugar melts off.
  4. Now deep fry the balls until they turn brown.
  5. Transfer them to the sugar solution and let them soak for about an hour.
