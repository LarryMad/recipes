Ingredients
1. 6 Yukon gold potatoes, about 6 ounces each
2. 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
3. 3 tablespoons olive oil
4. 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, plus more for garnish
5. Flaky sea salt, to taste

Directions
1. Preheat the oven to 425F
2. Slice the potatoes
3. Season the potatoes
4. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes
