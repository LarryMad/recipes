Ingredients:
 - 1 cup brewed coffee made from Cafe Bustello Espresso mix
 - 4 tablespoons CoffeeMate Natural Bliss Hazelnut flavored creamer
 - 1 tablespoon organic whole milk
 - 1 teaspoon sugar
 - 1/4 cup ice, cubed

 Directions:
  1. Add ice, creamer, milk and sugar to empty 12oz cup.

  2. Pour coffee into the cup.

  3. Stir and enjoy!
