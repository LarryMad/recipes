INGREDIENTS
700 grams / 1 1/2 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized chunks
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, more if you love heat
1 red capsicum / bell pepper, diced
1 onion, diced

TO SERVE
8 tortillas (flour or corn), heat to packet instructions

OPTIONAL TOPPINGS
Lettuce
Avocado
Salsa
Shredded cheese
Greek yoghurt
Lime wedges
Chopped Coriander

INSTRUCTIONS

OVEN METHOD
Preheat the oven to 180C / 350F.
Place chicken, capsicum / bell pepper and onion on a baking tray.  Sprinkle spices and oil on top, then mix well with your hands until all pieces are coated.
Pop the tray in the oven for 15-20 minutes until chicken in cooked through, giving the mix a stir half way. 
Assemble the tacos as you wish and enjoy! 

STOVETOP METHOD
Add spices, oil and chicken into a bowl and mix until chicken is evenly coated.  Heat a frying pan on medium-high heat.  Cook the chicken in two batches, turning each batch halfway, until the chicken is cooked through (approx. 5-8 mins each batch).  Place cooked chicken in a clean bowl.  
Pour a little more oil to then pan, then add onion and capsicum and cook, stirring regularly, until onion is softened (approx 1-2 mins).  Add cooked chicken back to the pan, mix together with onion and capsicum then remove from heat.  
Assemble the tacos as you wish and enjoy! 

NOTES
TORTILLAS -  Feel free to use whichever tortillas you prefer - crunchy or soft, corn or flour - it all works.
