Sugar Cookies;
350g softened butter
2 cups white sugar
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
5 cups plain flour
1 pinch of salt
In a large bowl, beat together butter and sugar together first until smooth and creamy. 
Then beat in eggs and vanilla. Stir through flour and salt.
Cover, and chill dough in the fridge for an hour or two before rolling it out.
Bake on 180 for about 10~15mins, depending on cookie size.
Royal Icing;
1 1/2 cups of sifted icing sugar
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
1 eggwhite
Lightly whisk eggwhite and lemon juice together in a bowl. 
Gradually add icing sugar, whisking until smooth and combined. 
Double recipe as needed, basic amount makes enough to ice about a dozen cookies.
