Hoppen John
Yield: 3 servings

Ingredients and directions for this recipe
4 sl Bacon, diced 1 c Hot water
1 md Onion, chopped 1 ts Salt
16 oz Black eyed peas 1/4 ts Pepper
1 c Instant rice 

Cook bacon in microwave till crisp, add onion and cook. Mix all
ingredients and cook in microwave on high for 8 to 10 minutes. Let
stand 5 to 10 minutes. Randy Rigg
