HOTCAKES RECIPE

INGREDIENTS:
- hotcakes flour
- milk
- eggs
- butter
- mapple syrup
- watter
- oil


STEPS:

1. Sift the flour and baking powder into a medium mixing bowl.
   Stir in the sugar. Combine the eggs, butter, milk and vanilla in a jug.

2. Hear a medium non-stick frypan over medium to low heat.
   Lightly oil cooking spray.

3. Serve hotcakes with extra butter and maple syrup.
