Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup sugar
 - 2/3 cup cream
 - 3 cups powdered sugar
 - 1/3 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, stir sugar, cream, and milk.
  2. Heat on low until mix is hot and foam begins to appear around edges.
  3. Transfer to container and stir in vanilla.
  4. Chill for at least several hours.
  5. Churn mix for 20 minutes. Serve or place in freezer.
