Indian Tea ('Chai')

Bring 1 cup water and 2 tea spoons of tea leaves to boil in a pot. Add 1/2 cup of milk, 1 spoon of sugar(if needed), 'Indian Chai Masala' powder and keep stirring 
for 1-2 min. Bring to a boil once again. Pour the tea into your favorite cup while straining out the tea leaves. 


Get a good book to read and enjoy it with your 'Chai'! :)
