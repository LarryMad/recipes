Jalapeno Pepper Dip

1 cup mayonnaise
2X 8oz packages cream cheese
1X 4oz can diced (or fresh) green chilis, drained
2oz canned jalapenos, diced and drained (or fresh, put more if you like spicy)
1.5cup shredded Parmesan cheese
couple of handfuls of dried breadcrumbs(plain)

------------------------------------------------------------
Mix cream cheese and mayonnaise in a bowl(with a mixer until smooth). 
Add diced chilis and  jalapenos, mix.
Microwave for ~3min until hot,mix in-between.
Top with Parmesean cheese and bread crumbs evenly.
Broil 2-3min.

Enjoy!
