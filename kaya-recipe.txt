Ingredients:
  - 4 or 5 eggs
  - 200g (7oz) sugar
  - 1/2 cup coconut cream
  - 3/4 cup coconut milk
  - 3 pandan leaves, tie into knot
  - 1 1/2 tablesppons corn starch + 1 1/2 tablespoons water

  Caramel:
  50g - 75g (1.5oz to 2.5oz) sugar

Directions:
1) Crack the eggs into big bowl or container, followed by coconut cream, coconut milk and sugar.   Whisk well, and then filter the milk with strainer.

2) Transfer the egg mixture into a sauce pan (non-stick preferred).  Add the pandan leaves into egg mixture and turn on the heat to medium low.
