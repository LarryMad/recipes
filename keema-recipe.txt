1. Get 2 tablespoon of oil and put it on stove.
2. Add zeera when oil is hot.
3. Add chopped onion and cook it for 3-4 minute.
4. Add lettuce, green pepper, and some other vegetable (whatever you want) and cookt it for 10 minutes.
5. Add spices (mirchi, dhaniya, keema-masala, salt) and cook it for 2-3 mins.
6. Finally Add the eggs and cook it for 3 mins. 
That's it keema is ready.
