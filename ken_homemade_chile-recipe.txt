1. jar of home canned tomatos
2. 1/2 dried habenero pepper
3. 2 tbsp of chili powder
4. 1 tsp of kosher salt
(Blend in blender)

5. 1/2 white or yellow onion
6. 1 lb of deer burger
(Brown in bottom of large pot)

7. Once the onions and deer burger are browned, add the contents of the blender to the pot. Bring to light boil and let simmer for 10 minutes.
8. Add 3 cans of chili beans. Stir into pot and bring to light boil and let simmer for 30 minutes. Make to stir pot at least every 3 minutes so the chili will not burn.
