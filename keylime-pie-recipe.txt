Key Lime Pie

Ingredients

1 (14-oz.) can sweetened condensed milk
1 tablespoon loosely packed lemon zest
1/2 cup Key lime juice
3 large egg yolks $
1/4 cup buttermilk $
Buttery Cracker Crust, baked
Vegetable cooking spray $
Sweetened Whipped Cream $

Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 325°. Whisk together first 3 ingredients in a bowl.

2. Beat egg yolks with a handheld mixer in a medium bowl at high speed 4 to 5 minutes or
until yolks become pale and ribbons form on surface of mixture when beater is lifted.
Gradually whisk in sweetened condensed milk mixture, and whisk until thoroughly
combined; whisk in buttermilk. Pour mixture into prepared crust.

3. Bake at 325° for 20 to 25 minutes or until set around edges. (Pie will be slightly jiggly.)
Cool on a wire rack 1 hour. Cover pie with lightly greased (with cooking spray) plastic wrap,
and freeze 4 to 6 hours.

4. Top with Sweetened Whipped Cream.
