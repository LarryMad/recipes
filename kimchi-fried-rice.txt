Ingredients
- Ground meat 
- Chopped kimchi
- Canola oil 
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sesame oil
- Gochujang
- Rice
- Chopped garlic
- 1 egg
- 1 cup water

Directions
1. Season ground meat with salt and pepper
2. Brown the meat on high heat then add the chopped kimchi and cook for 1 minute
3. Add rice, water, and gochujang then mix well
4. When water reduces, add sesame oil and mix
5. Fry an egg on a separate pan
6. Add the egg on top of the fried rice and mix
