Ingredients:
 - 1 liter of water
 - 1 cups sugar
 - 1 teaspoons corn syrup
 - 4 teaspoons lemon juce
 - 4 Cordemon powedered
 - 4 Blask pepper powedered
 - 1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
 1. Mix all ingredients in a large jug
 2. Stir to mix sugar and all ingredients
 3. Add some ice to cool, or keep in refrigerator
 4. Filter and serve the lemonade
