1 cup dried brown lentils
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 cups water
4 tablespoons of olive oil
1 inch ginger, diced fine
1 bunch spinach chopped a little
1 bunch cilantro, chopped
juice from 1/2 lemon

1. pick over lentils and boil in water until tender (about 45 minutes).

2. heat oil in pan and fry ginger for 30 seconds

3. add cilantro and cook` until wilted

4. add spinach and cook until wilted

5. add cooked lentils with a bit of cooking liquid. Allow to simmer
   for 10 minutes

6. Squeeze lemon juice into spinach and lentils
