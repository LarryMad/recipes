Ingredients:
 - 4 oz pasta
 - 4 oz evaporated milk
 - 4 oz cheese
 - 1/4 tsp chili flakes

Directions:
 1. Add pasta to pot and cover with water
 2. Add chili flakes
 3. Bring to boil and cook until pasta is al dente and water is almost evaporated
 4. Add cheese and evaporated milk
 5. You're done!
