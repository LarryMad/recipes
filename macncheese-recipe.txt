Mac n Cheese
Serves up to 4
For four Ounces of Macaroni

Prepare one small sauce pot.
Fill with 24oz of water, and 12oz of milk
Bring to a boil and and macaroni, reduce heat to medium low.
Cook till almost all liquid evaporates.
Add 4oz of any american cheese, and 8oz of your cheese of choice(I choose cheddar)
Remove from heat stir till combined serve.
