Title: Manhattan (Cocktail)
From: http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/ted-allen/classic-manhattan-cocktail-recipe.html

Ingredients:
    - Ice
    - 2 parts whiskey
    - 1 part sweet vermouth
    - 1 to 2 dashes bitters, such as Angostura
    - orange peel
    - real maraschino cherries (see recipe)

Directions:
    - Place ice in cocktail shaker
    - Add whiskey, vermouth and bitters
    - Rub the orange peel around the rim of the cocktail glass
    - Strain the drink into the glass
    - Add 1 to 2 real maraschino cherries
