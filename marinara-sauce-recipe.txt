Ingredients:
 - 1/4 cup of olive oil
 - 1 red onion, chopped
 - 6 garlic cloves, sliced
 - 2 cans crushed tomatoes (perferably san marzano)
 - 1 tsp salt
 - 6 fresh basil leaves, sliced

Directions:
 1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan.

 2. Add onion and garlic to the pan.  Cook 8-10 minuetsm or until golden brown.

 3. Add tomatoes and salt.

 4. Bring to a bubble, cover, lower the heat to a simmer, and cook for 30 minutes.

 5. Remove from heat and add the fresh basil.

 6. Enjoy!
