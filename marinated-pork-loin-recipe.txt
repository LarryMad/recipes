Ingredient List
·8 Slices Pork Loin 
·1/2 C. Soy Sauce 
·6 T. Brown Sugar 
·1/2 C. Olive Oil	
·2 t. Cumin 
·1 t. Dried Mustard 
·2 t. Paprika 
·1/2 C. Cilantro

Instructions
Marinate pork loin with ingredients listed above for about two hours. 
Grill for about 5 minutes per side. 
Serve with potatoes, cous cous, etc.
