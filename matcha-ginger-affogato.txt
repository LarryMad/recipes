Ingredients
    - 2 teaspoons (about 3-4 g) matcha green tea powder
    - ¾ teaspoon powdered ginger
    - 6 tablespoons (90 ml) hot water (at 175ºF)
    - ½ cup (120 ml) vanilla ice cream (regular or dairy-free)

Instructions
    Combine ginger and matcha in a small-ish bowl. Add a few drops of hot water
    and whisk to form a paste. Gradually add the rest of the hot water, whisking
    until smooth. Pour matcha over ice cream and serve.
