Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds ground beef
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 1 small onion chopped
 -2 jalapeno pepper chopped
 - One 4 oz tomato pesto
 - One 4 oz salsa verde
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - One 8-ounce can black beans, drained and rinsed
 - One 8-ounce can pinto beans, drained and rinsed

Directions:
 1. Brown the ground beef in a large bot and drain off the excess fat.
 2. Add the tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 5. Add the beans and simmer for 30 more minutes.
