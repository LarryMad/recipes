Ingredients:
 - 3 table spoons oil
 - 1 onion
 - 3 green chili peppers
 - 3 tomatoes
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon black pepper (optional)
 - 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
 - 100 gram tofu

Directions:
 1. Put the oil in a pan and add the chopped onions and sliced peppers. Let them fry until the onions are pink.
 
 2. Add the chopped tomatoes, salt, and the peppers. Close the pan with a lid and wait for it to cook until the tomatoes melt completely.
 
 3. Add the the tofu by crushing with your hand and mix for a couple of minutes.
