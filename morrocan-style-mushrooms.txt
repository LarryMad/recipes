Ingredients

1 lb mushrooms, washed and sliced (I used pre-sliced mushrooms, 8 oz. regular and 8 oz. portabella, soaked twice and drained)
4 T. extra-virgin olive oil
3-4 garlic cloves, crushed
salt
2 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. chili powder
Juice of 1/2 lemon
3 T. finely chopped cilantro or parsley


Heat oil in large frying pan til hot, add mushrooms and garlic and fry quickly. 
Add salt, cumin, chili powder, and lemon juice and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, for 10-20 minutes. 
Add cilantro or parsley towards the end. Serve cold, warm or at room temperature.
