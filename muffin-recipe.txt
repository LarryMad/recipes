Ingredients:
 - 1 egg
 - 120ml milk
 - 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
 - 200g plain flour
 - 100g caster sugar
 - 2 teaspoons baking powder
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 200 C / Gas 6. Grease muffin tin or use bun paper cases.
 2. Beat egg with a fork.
 3. Then stir in milk and oil.
 4. Sift flour into a large bowl.
 5. Add sugar, baking powder and salt.
 6. Add egg mixture to flour and stir until flour is moistened. BATTER SHOULD BE LUMPY. DO NOT OVERMIX.
 7. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full. Bake 20 to 25 minutes until golden brown.
