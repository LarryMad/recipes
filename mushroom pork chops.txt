Ingredients:
4 pork chops
salt and pepper
1 pinch garlic salt
1 onion chopped
1/2 pound fresh mushroom,sliced
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup

step1: Season pork chops with salt, pepper, and garlic salt to taste.

step 2: In a large skillet, brown the chops over medium-high heat. Add the onion and mushrooms, and saute for one minute. Pour cream of mushroom soup over chops. Cover skillet, and reduce temperature to medium-low. Simmer 20 to 30 minutes, or until chops are cooked through.
