Mushroom Hamburger Surprise

ingredients:
1 kg ground beef, not too lean
2 cans Campbell's Mushroom Soup
1/2 tsp dijon mustard
1/4 tsp prepared horse-radish
garlic powder

method:
pre-heat oven to 325F
in a low baking dish, mix ground beef, soup, mustard and horse-radish 
sprinkle with garlic powder to taste
bake for one hour

the surprise is that it is so good!

best served with baked russet potatoes, creamed corn, green peas
and a red bordeaux,such as st.emilion or pomerol
