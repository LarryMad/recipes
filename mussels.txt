Ingredients:
- Fresh mussels
- Olive oil
- 2 shallots or a small onion, finely chopped
- Dry white wine or dry cider
- Mixed herbs and ground black pepper

Directions:
1. Clean the outside of the mussell shells. Make sure that all the shells are closed, discard any open ones.

2. Fry the shallots or onions in the olive oil until soft.

3. Add the wine or cider and bring to the boil. Add the mixed herbs and pepper

4. Add the mussels and cook for about 4-5 minutes until all the shells open

5. Serve in a bowl and pour some of the liquid over the mussels.
