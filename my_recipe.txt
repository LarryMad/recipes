recipe for strawberry banana milk shake:

Ingredients:
1 banana (cut into small roll slices)
4-5 ripe strawberries
1 spoon of honey
1 teaspoon of sugar
1 cup of milk

Method:
Put all the ingredients in blender; blend them well into a smooth milkshake.
Serve it chilled. Yay !! the milkshake is ready to be enjoyed!
