NACHOS AND CHEESE
1 BAGS OF NACHOS
1 CUBE (8 TBLS) SALTED BUTTER
8 TBLS FLOUR
4 CUPS OF MILK
1 CUBE OF VELVETTA
1/2 JAR JALAPENO

MELT BUTTER OVER MEDIUM HEAT IN 3QT POT
SLOWLY ADD FLOUR UNTIL SMOOTH
SLOWLY ADD MILK TILL CREAMY 
ADD CUBED VELVETTA AND SIMMER STIRRING CONSTANTLY UNTIL SMOOTH
REMOVE FROM HEAT AND ADD JALAPENOS
POUR OVER CHIPS
