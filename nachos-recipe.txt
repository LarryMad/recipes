Ingredients:
- Nachos
- Shredded Cheese
- Jalepenos
- Refried Beans
- Shredded chicken or ground beef
- Sour Cream


Directions:
1. Prepare refried beens and meat according to package instructions.

2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

3. Cover the bottom of a large casserole dish with nachos then spread refried
beans, meat, jalepenos, and cheese over the nachos.

4. Bake 10-15 minutes or until a cheese is warmed through.

5. Enjoy!
