Ingredients:
 - 1 Jello No Bake Cheesecake mix
 - 1 graham cracker pie crust
 - 1 package of raspberries
 - lemon or lemon juice
 - sugar
 - water

Directions:
 1. Mix the Jello No Bake Cheescake mix with water as directed by the packaging, 
 	making sure to whisk thoroughly.
 
 2. As the mix sets, pour the contents into the pie crust.
 
 3. Heat the raspberries in a non-stick pan on low heat until the raspberries break
 	down and turn into a sauce like consistency. Add lemon and sugar to taste.

 4. Take the raspberry sauce and drizzle over the cheesecake and pie crust.
