Ingredients:
 - 50g Flour
 - 1/3 cup milk
 - 1 egg
 
 Note:  Ingredients can be multiplied to make more pancakes.

Directions:
 1. Pre-heat round (around 12 inch) pan to medium high heat.  Spray with Pam
    or use another non-stick technique.
 
 2. Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix with a wisk.
 
 3. Pour 1/3 cup of batter on pan tilt pan in all directions to make a thin
    layer.  In about 2-4 minutes, flip the pancake.
    
 4. Serve with toppings such as jam, butter, sugar, etc.  Roll or fold the
    pancake and enjoy!
