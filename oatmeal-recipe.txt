Ingredients:
  - 60 grams of Quaker oats
  - 240 grams of milk
  - 1 serving of desired berries
  - 1 serving of desired nuts
  - Any amount of brown sugar
  - 1 cinnamon stick

Instructions:

1.) Put the oats, milk, and cinnamon stick in one bowl
2.) Place the bowl in the microwave and set it for 2 minutes and 20 seconds.
3.) Wash berries
4.) When the microwave finishes, add the berries, nuts and brown sugar. Dispose
    the cinnamon stick.
5.) Enjoy
