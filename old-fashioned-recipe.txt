Ingredients:
 - 2 oz whiskey (rye or bourbon)
 - 1 sugar cube
 - 2-3 dashes Angostura bitters
 - splash soda water (optional)
 - ice

Directions:
 1. Place sugar cube in cup and add bitters.
 
 2. Muddle sugar cube until dissolved (or at least completely broken up).
 
 3. Add ice, whiskey, and soda water if desired. Stir gently.
