Ingredients:
 - 1 sugar cube
 - 3 dashes of ang. bitters
 - 3 dashes of orange bitters
 - 2 ounces whisky
 - slice of orange peel
 - slice of lemon peel
 - 1 block ice cube

Directions:
 1. muddle sugar and bitters

 2. add whisky and stir together with ice

 3. add ice cube block

 4. squeeze oil from orange and lemon peels into glass, then add peels to glass
