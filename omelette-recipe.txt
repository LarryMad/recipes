Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 tablespoon of coconut oil
 - 2 cherry tomatos 
 - 1 slice of cheese
 - 1/2 teaspoon of salt


Directions:

 - pour coconut oil into the pan, and heat it on the stove with medium heat
 - add the eggs to a bowl
 - add salt to the bowl
 - stir the eggs until they are mixed
 - when the coconut oil melts, distribute it on the pan
 - pour the eggs into the pan
 - slice the cherry tomatoes to small pieces, and add them to the pan
 - chunk the cheese into small pieces, and add them to the pan
 - let the eggs cook for about 3-5 minutes, and then flip the egg to cook the other side for about 30 seconds
 - pour your meal and enjoy
