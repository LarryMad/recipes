Ingredients:
- 4 eggs
- Dash of milk
- Whatever you want inside (cheese, vegetable, meat etc.)

Directions:
1. Beat eggs together in bowl
2. Prepare food to add(ie meat, vegetables)
3. Add milk
4. Butter pan
5. Pour eggs into pan
6. Cook till egg begins to set, then draw set egg into middle of pan
7. Let liquified egg go to pan edges
8. Let set, then add ingredients
9. Fold or roll eggs
