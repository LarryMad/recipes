Pancake Recipe

Ingredients
 - pancake mix from store
 - an egg or two
 - apples
 - splash of milk
 - that's probably it

 1. Read the instructions on the box
 2. Skin/peel apples
 3. Cut up apples
 4. Put in apples
 5. Stir and what-not
 6. Pour onto greased griddle
 7. Impatiently wait for them to be done
 8. Don't forget to flip them
 9. Pour generous amounts of syrup on them
