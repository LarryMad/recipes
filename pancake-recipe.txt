Pancakes

1 cup of milk
2 tbsp vinegar

1 cup flour
1 tsp baking sode
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 salt
pinch pumpkin spice

2 egg
2 tbsp melted butter

combine milk and vinegar and let them sit for 5 minutes
combine dry ingredients
mix egg and butter and then combine with milk
stir dry ingredients into wet ingredients
pour 1/4 cup of batter into warmed pan and flip after 2 minutes
