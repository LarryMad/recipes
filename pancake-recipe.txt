Ingredients:
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1/2 cup canola oil
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 cup of crushed walnuts

Directions:
1. Turn on stove to med-high heat. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, salt.  Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla.  Add crushed. Mix until smooth.  

2. Pour a little bit of canola oil per pancake made. Pour batter into prepared hot pan. Cook until edges are brown. Flip.

3. Serve with cut strawberries and bananas.
