Ingredients:

 - 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
 - 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 tablespoon white sugar
 -  1/4 cups milk
 - 1 egg
 - 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well 
in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the 
batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both 
sides and serve hot.
