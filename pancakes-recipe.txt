Pancake Recipe

Ingredients
- 1 cup plain flour
- 1 egg
- pinch of salt
- 200mL milk
- 100mL water

Steps
1) Whiz up all of it til smooth
2) Wait for 10 mins, let mature
3) Heat frying pan to highest heat
4) Add small amount of oil
5) When smoking, make thin pancake
6) When brown, flip
7) Take off when brown dots appear
