For pancakes you will need:
1. Break 2 eggs into bowl, add 3 tablespoons of sugar and
	1 teaspoon of salt, whisk it to foamed state
2. Add 1 cup of milk, mix it up, carefully add 1 cup of baking flour,
	constantly stirring the dough
3. Repeat previous point
4. Add 1/4 cup of olive oil and a teaspoon of baking soda foam
5. Stir it thoroughly and let it rest for at least 10 minutes
6. Heat up the pan and fry the pancake until bubbles of air start to
	appear on its surface. Flip it to the other side and fry for 20 
	seconds more.
7. That's it
