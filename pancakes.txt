Buttermilk Pancakes 
 
FULL BATCH
4 Eggs
4 cups buttermilk
1/2 cup oil
3 1/2 cup flour (17.5 oz.)
1/4 cup sugar (2 oz.)
4 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons soda
2 teaspoons salt

 
HALF BATCH
2 Eggs
2 cups buttermilk
1/4 cup oil
1 3/4 cup flour (8.75 oz.)
1/4 cup sugar (1 oz.)
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon soda
1 teaspoon salt
 
Heat griddle to medium high heat.   In a large bowl, beat eggs; stir in buttermilk and oil.  Add remaining ingredients and stir just until large lumps disappear.  Lightly grease heated griddle.  Pour 1/4 cup batter on griddle to form pancake.  Bake until bubbles at edges start to dry; turn and bake other side

Makes 32 pancakes (16 for half batch)

Source: Pillsbury Kitchens Cookbook p. 99
