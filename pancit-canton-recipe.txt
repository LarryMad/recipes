Pancit Canton Recipe

Ingredients:
* Pancit Canton Oriental Style Noodles 454 g (16 oz) Family Pack
* 1/1 lb Pork Belly (cut into small bite-sized pieces)
* 1/2 lb Shrimp
* Half Cabbage
* 2 medium-sized carrots
* About 2 handfuls Snow Peas
* Medium-sized red Onions
* 3 cloves Garlic
* 3 tbsp Soy sauce + 1/2 tbsp Soy sauce
* 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
* 1/2 tsp Salt
* 1/2 tsp Ground Black pepper
* 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil for the shrimp
* 3 cups Water

Procedure:
* Cook shirmp in medium heat add salt and pepper. Remove when it is golden brown
* On the same pan cook pork belly. Cook until brown or fats starting to come out
* Saute onion and garlic. After a few minutes add soy sauce, water and carrots
* Cover the pan and simmer for 10 minutes on medium heat.
* Add snow peas, cabbage, shrimp. Cook for 2-3 minutes on medium heat
* Add half of the noodles then mix. After that add the other half and cover and cook for 5 minutes in medium heat.
* After 5 minutes turn heat to high then mix for 2-3 minutes.
* Done
