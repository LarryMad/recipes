Ingredients:
 - 2 - 3 potatoes
- red chillies
- 2 - 3 tomatoes
- olive oil

Directions:
 1.  Cut the potatoes

 2. Cut the tomatoes and fry them.

 3. Add olive oil to a frying pan and heat it
 
 4. Cut some red chillies in slices, add them to the oil and fry them

 5. Remove the chillies and fry the potatoes using that oil.
 
 6. Mix the potatoes and the fried tomato.
