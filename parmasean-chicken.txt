Ingredients:
- 4 pieces of Boneless Chicken Breast ( Portion is upto you)
- Small jar of Mayonnaise
- Bread crumbs (of your choice)
- 1 Cup of Parmasean Cheese

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Mix Mayo & Parmasean Cheese together
3. Rub mayo mixed w/ parmasean cheese onto the pieces of chicken
4. Sprinkle the bread crumbs over the rubbed chicken pieces
5. Place chicken pieces in oven for about 40 min to 45 min.
