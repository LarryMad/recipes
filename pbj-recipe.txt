Ingredients: fresh ground peanut butter
             pumpernickel bread - 2 slices
             grape or currant jelly
             Finlandia Finnish Swiss cheese - 2 slices

Spread the jelly *thinly* on one slice of the bread (or thickly if you must)
Spread the peanut butter on the other slice
Place the Swiss cheese on top of either the peanut butter or the jelly
Combine into your sandwich

This sandwich is called a Hubert Humphrey, since it was his favorite sandwich!
