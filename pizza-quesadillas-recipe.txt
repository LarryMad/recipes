Ingredients:
 - 1 small can mushrooms, drained (4 oz)
 - 2 tbs olive oil
 - 1 tbs balsamic vinegar
 - 1/8 tsp basil (or 2 leaves fresh, chopped)
 - 2 cloves garlic, crushed
 - salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
 1. To assemble pizza, place ingredients on top of 1 tortilla.  Top with the other tortilla.
 2. Heat about 1 tsp olive oil in a 10-inch skillet over medium heat.  
 3. Place “pizza” in pan and saute for about 5 minutes (bottom tortilla should start to get crispy)
 4. Turn and saute for another 5 minutes.
 5. Use a sharp knife or pizza cutter to cut into quarters.  Serve immediately.
