Ingredients:
 - 500g Flour
 - 100ml Water
 - 10g yeast
 - 100ml seaved tomatoes
 - 1 Spoon tomato paste
 - some oregano
 - Toppings you like
 
Directions
 1. Water and yeast, until the yeast is solved in the water.
 2. Mix the water with the flour.
 3. Knead the dough until it is soft. It should be elastic
    but not sticky. If it is to dry, add water, else add flour.
 4. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes then knead it.
    Repeat this step at least 3 times.
 5. Roll out the dough on a backing-paper.
 6. Mix the tomatoes with oregano an tomato-paste and
    spread it across the dough.
 7. Add the toppings.
 8. Put into oven at about 200Â°C for 20 to 30 minutes.
