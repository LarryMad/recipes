Ingredients:
-14 oz package prebaked pizza crust
-1 cup diced cooked chicken breast
-3 tablespoons buffalo wing sauce
-1/2 cup buffalo wing sauce
-4 oz package crumbled blue cheese
-1 stalk celery, thinly sliced
-1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 475F and line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
2. Place pizza crust on prepared baking sheet.
3. Mix chicken and 3 tablespoons wing sauce together in a bowl until evenly coated.
4. Spread 1/2 cup wing sauce on the pizza crust; top with blue cheese, chicken mixture, and celery.
5. Cover pizza with mozzarella cheese.
6. Bake in preheated oven until pizza is cooked through and cheese is bubbling, about 12 minutes.
7. Cool pizza for 5 minutes before cutting.
