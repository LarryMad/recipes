Pizza Lassagna

1. Open two cans of bisquits and cut each bisquit into fourths and roll each forth into a ball. 
2. Coat the balls in parmesian and garlic powder and place inside a large glass dish.
3. Bake for 5 min at 400 degrees.
4. Add pizza sauce, cheese, ground beef, and pepperoni to the top.
5. Bake for 15 - 20 min.
6. ENJOY!
