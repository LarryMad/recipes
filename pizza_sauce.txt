1 med onion, chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 Tbsp olive oil

14 oz. can crushed tomatoes

14 oz. can diced tomatoes

8 oz. can tomato sauce

6 oz. can tomato paste

1/4 cup red wine

1 tsp  salt

1/2 tsp parsley

1 tsp oregano

1/4 tsp black pepper

1/4 tsp crushed red pepper

1/4 tsp basil

Saute onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat until onion is translucent. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or until sauce has desired consistency.
