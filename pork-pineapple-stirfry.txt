Ingerdients:
	1lb Pork
	1 can Pineaaple (with Juice)
	1/2 Onion
	1/2 cup of diced Mushrooms
	3 cups of Brocolii
	1 cup of Quinoa

Directions:
	1.) Sautee the pork in a large stirfry pan. Place aside.
	
	2.) Sautee the brocolii, pineapples (with juice),onions, and mushrooms

	3.) Combine pork and cook on medium heat.

	4.) Season to taste.
