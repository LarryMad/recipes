separate ribs into pieces
bread them with your favorite bread crumbles
place the ribs into airfryer, one layer only with no overlapps
set airfry at 360 for 15 mins
turn each piece of ribs and airfry at 360 for another 5 mins
it is done
note: 1. you may want to adjust the time based on the wattage and the amount of 
ribs you have
      2. you may boil the ribs on a stove for 5-10 mins if you prefer
