INGREDIENTS:
3-4 potatoes
1 spoon salt
2 onions
1/2 garlic
2-3 slices cheese

METHOD:
boil the potatoes, them mash them, add salt, mashed onions and garlic paste and mash
the mixture. make small potato balls and put a small slice of cheeze in each ball.
warn the oil, put each ball in the mashed egg and apply bread chrumps, then fry the
ball for 2 mins. Garnish them in the end.
