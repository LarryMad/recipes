4 potetoes
a glass of milk
50 gr sausage
50 gr mortadella
100 gr mozzarella cheese
50 gr butter

Boil down four poteatos, then remove peel and split them with a fork in
a bowl.
Add the milk, then chop sausage, mozzarella and mortadella and add them.
Mix with a spoon.

Finally use butter on the stove, put the mix in the stove and cook in oven for
half an hour at 200 C degrees.
