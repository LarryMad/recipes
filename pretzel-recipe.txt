Chocolate covered pretzels
Ingredients:
Chocolate melting disc
Tiny twist pretzels

Instructions
1.  Put chocolate discs in microwaveable bowl and microwave on 50% until melted
2.  Dip pretzels in chocolate and place on a tray covered with waxed paper
3.  When the tray is full, put it in the refrigerator for 10 minutes
4.  Remove pretzels from the tray and put in a container.
