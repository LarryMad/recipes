Ingredients:
- 1 corn or flour tortilla
- 1/3 cup shredded or thinly sliced cheese of your choice
Optional:
- avocado
- guacamole
- sour cream
- refried beans
- grilled steak or chicken

Directions:
  1.  Warm up tortilla in a griddle over medium heat.
  2.  Add cheese and optional ingredients to tortilla and fold in half.  If using avocado, guacamole, or sour cream, add these after step 3.
  3.  Heat quesadilla in griddle until cheese is melted.
