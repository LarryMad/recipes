Ingredients:
 - 1/4 cup quinoa
 - 1/4 low fat cheddar cheese
 - 1/2 cup water

Directions:
 1. Combine water and quinoa in a rice cooker.

 2. Use your device's setting for white rice to cook the quinoa.

 3. When the timer indicates the food is ready, transfer the cooked quinoa to a bowl.

 4. Mix the cheese into the quinoa while it is still hot.

 5. Allow 5 minutes to cool before enjoying your sustainable, low-fat, gluten-free, high-protein meal.
