Ingredients:
 - 5 fl.oz natural yoghurt
 - 5 fl.oz double cream
 - brown sugar
 - 1 lb raspberries

Directions:
 1. Wash the raspberries and drain very well. Divide into 4 glass bowls.

 2. Beat together the yoghurt and cream until firm enough to hold peaks.

 3. Spoon the mixture over the raspberries to completely cover them.

 4. Sprinkle brown sugar over the top.

 5. Cover with cling film and refrigerate for 24 hours.
