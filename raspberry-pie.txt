Use Joy Of Cooking basic pie dough.

Filling amount for 7" pie pan with double crust.

Combine in a bowl:
3 C raspberries
juice from 1/2 medium lemon
1/2 C sugar
1/4 C small pearl tapioca
Then let sit for 15 minutes, periodically stirring gently.

Preheat oven to 375F degrees.

Roll out bottom and top crusts of pie dough. Fit bottom crust in 7" pan.
Add filling. Install top crust; securely attach and add vents.

Place pie in oven. Cook for 30 minutes.

Turn heat down to 325F degrees. Cook for another 25 minutes or until done.
