Sweet potato pizza:

1. Pre-heat the over to 200ÂºC
2. Mix in a bowl 2-3 cooked sweet potatos and mash them. Then add 1 cup of oat flour, a teaspoon of baking soda, a pinch of salt and other desired seasonings
3. Mix with your hands until forming a ball of pizza dough
4. Flatten the dough on a baking sheet until forming a disk about half an inch thick.
5. Leave in the oven for about 15 minutes
6. Take off of the oven and put tomato sauce and other desired toppings. Put in the oven again if necessary.
