Ingredients:
 - 1 package suasage of choice
 - 1 cup rice
 - 2 cups water
 - 2 cans Blue Runner red beans

 * Desired seasoning

Directions:
 1. Cook sausage until slightly brown in medium sized pot adding desired seasoning.

 2. Bring to boil, 1 cup rice and 2 cups water. Lower temperature and simmer 20 minutes.

 3. Add red beans to sausage and 1 can of water.

 4. Cook beans and sausage until beginning boil, remove from heat.
