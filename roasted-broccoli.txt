ingredients
 - broccoli
 - olive oil (lots)
 - salt (a bit)

directions
1. preheat oven to 450
2. line a baking pan with parchment paper
3. cut broccoli into pieces, roughly uniform size, put in a large bowl
4. pour olive oil, salt, stir/shake broccoli
5. bake for 20 minutes; broccoli should be blackened
