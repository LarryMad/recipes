Ingredients:
 - 75g wheat flour
 - 2 eggs
 - cooking oil
 - 1 Onions 
 - 1 tomato
 - Salt


Directions:
 1. Form a soft dough of the wheat using less water as possible,some cooking oil and salt that is elastic but not sticky
 2. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until it is smooth
 3. Using a frying pan, pour on it and them put the dough until for some few minutes for both side until it is ready
 4. Fry the eggs on the frying pan still.
 5. Add the fryed eggs and dough and fry them for just some few minutes.
 6. Add Sliced Onions and Sliced tomatoes and enjoy the taste.
