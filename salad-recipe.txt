Ingredients
3 cups boiled sweet corn kernels 
1/2 cup chopped spring onions 
1/2 cup capsicum cubes
1/2 cup tomato cubes
1 tbsp vinegar
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp mustard powder
1 tbsp sugar
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Method 
1.Combine the sweet corn, spring onions, capsicum and tomatoes in a deep bowl and mix well.
2.Pour the dressing over the salad, toss well and refrigerate for at least an hour.
3.Serve chilled.
