Ingredients
1 tbsp smoked paprika
2 tsp ground cumin
4 skinless salmon fillets
200g natural yogurt
1 garlic clove, crushed
2 ripe avocados, stoned, peeled and diced
1 red onion, finely chopped
2 large tomato, deseeded and finely chopped
2 limes, juice of 1, 1 cut into wedges
small pack coriander, chopped
8 taco shells

Directions:
Heat the grill to high and line a large baking tray with foil.
Mix the smoked paprika and cumin in a small bowl.
Rub the spices over the salmon fillets and put them on the baking tray.
Pop under the grill for 8-10 mins until cooked through.
