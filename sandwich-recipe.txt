Ingredients:

- 2 slices of rye bread
- 1 slice of habanero jack cheese
- some spinach
- some pre-cooked chicken
- some sun-dried tomatoes
- English mustard
- olive oil
- chili flakes
- oregano

Directions:

1. Heat a pan on medium heat
2. Assemble the sandwich
3. Brush one side with olive oil
4. Put the sandwich on the pan, olive oil-side down.  Brush the top with olive oil
5. After a few minutes, flip the sandwich
6. Plate and enjoy!
