Ingredients:
 - 4 lbs antelope
 - 1 lb pork fat
 - many secret spices
 
Directions:
 1. Cube antelope and pork and place in chest freezer to chill.
 
 2. Combine spices, mix well with cubed meat, place back in freezer. Place all the parts of the meat grinder in the freezer as well.
 
 3. Grind well-chilled (should hurt your hands to hold it) seasoned meat cubes twice through fine plate. Mix in about 1/4-1/2 cup water to bind, then stuff in standard casings.

 4. Smoke over hickory chips for about 1 - 1 1/2 hours (at about 200 F). Quench in cold water.
