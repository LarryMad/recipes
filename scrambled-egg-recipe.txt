Ingredients:
	- 3 eggs
	- butter

Directions:
	1. Separate 1 egg.  Put the yolk in a cold pan.
	2. Add the butter and the other two eggs to the cold pan.
	3. Put the pan on a high heat and stir constantly.
	4. As clumps start forming alternate the pan on and off the heat to prevent the eggs cooking 
	too quickly.
	5. Season and serve as preferred.
