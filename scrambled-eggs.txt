Ingredients:
 - 3 eggs
 - 1 whole onion
 - salt
 - pepper
 - 2 frankfurters

Directions:
 1. Heat up the frying pan (small fire) and add a little bit of vegetable oil.
 2. Crack the eggs, scramble them in a bowl, and salt and pepper.
 3. Cut up frankfurters and onion into small pieces, put them on the pan.
 4. After about 5 minutes of cooking them on small fire, pour the eggs inside and start to shuffle them with the contents of the frying pan.
 5. When eggs start to get more solid (but still a bit liquidy) - empty the contents on a plate of your choice, and voila!
