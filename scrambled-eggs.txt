Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 cup milk
 - knob of butter
 - Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
 1. Heat Milk and Butter in a non stick saucepan until it simmers
 2. Add the eggs, salt and pepper, and stir with a wooden spoon
 3. Keep on the the heat and stir until the eggs are a firm texture
 4. Drain and serve on a bed of toast. Yum!
