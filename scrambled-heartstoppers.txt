Ingredients:
 - 3 eggs
 - Your favorite cheese
 - 2 Tbsp Sour Cream
 - Your favorite hot sauce

Directions:
 1. Shred 1 1/2 cups of cheese.
 
 2. Set stovetop to medium.
 
 3. In a mixing bowl whip eggs and sour cream.  Pour
 	into pan.

 4. Scramble eggs for 4 minutes.  Add hot sauce to your liking. Serve.
