Ingredients
    - Eggs
    - Bread with crust
    - Salt
    
Directions
1) Take a slice of bread and take out a core - it will form scrambled eggs. Lightly fry it in a pan. 
2) In the resulting form, pour the egg, lightly salt it and fry only one side, but you can with two.

You can experiment with the ingredients, adding cheese or tomatoes.
