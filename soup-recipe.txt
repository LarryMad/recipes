Ingredients:
 - 8 oz fresh mushrooms
 - 2 tablespoons onions, chopped
 - 1-2 garlic clove, minced
 - 2 tablespoons butter
 - 2-3 tablespoons flour (separated)
 - 2 cups chicken broth
 - 1 cup light cream or 1 cup evaporated milk
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon pepper
 - 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
 1. Cut the mushrooms into slices.

 2. Melt butter in large frying pan. Add in onions, garlic, and mushrooms. Cook
 	until onions are soft.

 3. Blend in 2 tablespoons flour and stir.

 4. Add in the chicken broth and heat until slightly thickened while stirring
 	frequently.

 5. Stir cream with additional 1 tablespoon flour and seasonings. Add in cream
 	to soup. Heat to thicken while stirring frequently.
