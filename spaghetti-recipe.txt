Ingredients:
 - Tomato/basil sauce
 - Spaghetti noodles

Directions:
  1. Using a pot, boil enough water so that it can encompass the entire amount of spaghetti noodles you wish to cook.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the spaghetti noodles and cook for 10-12 minutes. Stir occassionally.
  3. After 10-12 minutes, remove spaghetti from water and put the pot back on the stove. Add tomato/basil sauce.
  4. Cook basil sauce for 20 seconds, then add spaghetti noodles.
  5. Turn off stove and serve.
