As made by Irish-American mother in the 1960's

o cooked white rice
o stewed tomatoes
o sliced hot dogs
o cheddar cheese

Mix rice, stewed tomatoes and hot dogs in casserole put cheddar cheese slice on top
Bake 1/2 or so at 350 F until hot dogs are cooked and rice heated up

Serve to four hungry children, tell them it's Spanish and they'll think they are eating fancy meal
