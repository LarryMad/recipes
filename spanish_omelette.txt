Spanish omelette

Ingredients:
-4/5 eggs
-4 potatoes medium size
-Half an onion
-Salt
-Oil

Process:
-Cut the potatoes in slices (not so thin not so thick)
-Cut the onion
-Add salt to them
-Fry at medium fire both potatoes and onion in a frying pan for about 15-20 minutes with the oil hot. Try to slice the potatos into small pieces.
-When finished, remove the ingredients from the frying pan as well as the oil.
