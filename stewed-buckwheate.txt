Ingredients(for two):
1. 1/2 cup of buckwheat
2. 3/2 cup water
3. 1 medium size carrot
4. 1 medium size onion
5. 1 small size zucchini
6. salt and papper to taste
7. 3 spoons of oil

Directions:
1. Put backwheat and water into a pan on a stove
2. Cut vegetables into small pieces and add into the pan
3. Add oil and salt into the mix
4. Simmer for 30 minutes until there is no water left, pan covered 
with lid
5. Serve with green salad and fresh tomatoes
