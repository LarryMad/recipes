Super Apple Bake 
(Gwenmar B&B near Brandon)

Ingredients:
Base:
3 Tbsp butter
6 apples, peeled and sliced
6 eggs
1½ c. milk
1 cup flour
3 Tbsp granulated sugar
1 tsp vanilla
½ tsp salt
½ tsp cinnamon

Topping:
¼ tsp. Cinnamon
3 T brown sugar

Directions:
Melt butter in 9”x13” pan, or 12” fluted quiche dish in 425 ° F oven for about 3 min.
Add apples and bake until butter is sizzling.  Blend other ingredients together and pour over apples.

Mix together and sprinkle over top before baking.

Bake at 425 ° for 20 min. or until puffed and golden.  
Serve immediately with maple syrup and whip cream.
