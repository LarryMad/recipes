Ingredients:
 - 5 cups rice
 - 10 sheets of seaweed
 - 1 packages of crab meat
 - 1 avocadio
 - 1 cucumber


Directions:
 1. Place rice on seaweed.
 2. Add the crab meat, avocado, and cucumber slices on the bottom of the rice patch.
 3. Starting from the bottom where the ingredients are, tightly roll upwards.
 4. Once rolled, make sure the end of the roll is secure.
 5. With the end face down, use a sharp knife to cut into six even pieces.
