Ingredients
Servings: 1
Salt to taste
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon seasoning sauce (eg. MAGGI®)
1/2 teaspoon hot sauce bottle (eg. Tabasco®)
1 egg 355ml beer, cold.

Directions:
Preparation: Ready in: 5 minutes
1. Frost with salt a brewer or large glass jar.
2. Add the lemon juice.
3. Add worcestershire sauce.
4. Add seasoning sauce and hot sauce; 
5. Mix well.
6. Pour the beer and stir carefully.
7. If desired, serve over ice cubes.
