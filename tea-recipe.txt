Ingredients:
   -2   teaspoonful sugar
   -100 ml of milk
   -50  ml of water
   -2   teaspoonful Tea leaves
   
Directions
  1. Add water to a bowl
  2. put the bowl on the gas and let it heated for 1 minute
  3. Add tea leaves and sugar to the bowl
  4. Now let the whole mixture gets mixed and gets heated for the next 1 minute
  5. Now add the milk to it and let it boil for next 1 minute
  6. switch of the gas and pour the tea in a cup and enjoy it
