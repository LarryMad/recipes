Ingredients:
 - 1 cup water
 - 1/2 cup soy sauce
 - 1/2 tablespoon ginger 
 - 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
 - 4 tablespoons brown sugar
 - drizzle of honey
 - cayenne pepper (to taste)
 - 1 1/2 tablespoons corn starch
 - 1/2 cup cool water

Directions:
 1. Add water, soy saucce, ginger, garlic powder, brown sugar, honey and cayenne pepper to a boiling pot
    and mix well
 2. Bring to a simmer.
 3. Mix cool water and cornstarch and add to mixture.
 4. Let sauce thicken and cool.

 Great for rice and stir-fry.
