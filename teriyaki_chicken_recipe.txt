Ingredients:
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 1 3/4 cups teryiki sauce
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 cup vinegar
 - 1/2 cup soy sauce
 - 2 cups diced chicken 
 - 1 cup boiling water

Directions:
 1. Grill chicken, add in water, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and teriyaki sauce.
 
 2. Cook until boiling. Stir.
 
 3. Add sugar.
