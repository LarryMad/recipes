Texas Caviar

1 can pinto beans
1 can garbanzo beans
1 can black eyed peas
1 can red beans
1 can black beans
1 jar pimentos
1 can shoepeg corn
1 cup chopped green pepper
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 tsp salt

-drain beans, corn and pimentos
-combine sugar, oil and vinegar in a bowl and microwave for 1.5 minutes
-mix beans and dressing together
-refrigerate overnight
