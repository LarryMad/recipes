Ingredients:
 - 2 cups cut green beans
 - 2 cups dark red kidney beans
 - 2 cups garbanzo beans
 - 1 medium red onion, chopped
 - 1 medium red pepper, chopped
 - 1/2 cup olive oil
 - 1/2 cup white balsamic vinegar
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
 1. Cook green beans till tender. Cook kidney beans. Cook garbanzo beans.

 2. Toss beans with onion and pepper.

 3. Add the oil, vinegar, salt and pepper. Mix all the ingredients.

 4. Chill at least 4 hours before serving. Flavor improves with marinating overnight.
