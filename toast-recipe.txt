Recipe to make toast: 

=====================

Step 1: have bread.

Step 2: slice bread if not already in sliced form.

Step 3: put one leaf of bread into microwave oven.

Step 4: turn oven on. 

Step 5: stand by oven to keep warm.

Step 6: once sufficiently warm turn on microwave oven on full for three minutes.

Step 7: the toast should have now formed, it may need longer though. It should be hot and moist to the touch.

Step 8: add dry gravy granuals to serve. 

Step 9: become satiated. 


Serveing suggestion: a cold breeze.
