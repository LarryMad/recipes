Tofu Eggs Scrambled

1 block extra firm tofu
1 Tbsp Himilayan black salt
1 tsp tumeric
1 tsp oilve or non-stick spray


Steps

Use knife to shave off pieces of the tofu until the entire tofu is turned into a pile of shavings.

Use either non-stick spray or olive oil and coat a large fry pan and heat to medium-high heat.

Put tofu in pan and sprinkle salt and tumeric over tofu.

Gently mix up the salt and tumeric with the tofu until evenly distributed and tofu has the yellow
appearance of scrambled eggs.

Cook for a few minutes until the tofu has dried out some and is hot.

Serve.
