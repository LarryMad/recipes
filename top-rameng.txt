Ingredients:
 - 2 packets of top ramen beef flavored
 - 1 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 1 egg
 - 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
 - 2 tablespoons chooped onion
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 - 1 teaspoon salt or soy sause
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 
Directions:
 1. cook ramen in sause pan, drain extra water, add flavor packets
 2. Add garlic, egg, cilantro, cumin and soy or salt
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for 2 minutes
 4. ladel desired amount into bowl.
 5. top with cilantro and onion for a nice chrunch
