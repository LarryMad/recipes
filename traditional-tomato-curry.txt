
Recipe Name: Traditional Tomato Curry
Cuisine: Indian
Preparation Time: 30 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:
- 6 ripe tomatoes
- 2 yellow or red onions
- 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
- 1 tsp red chilli powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- dash of turmeric powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp vegetable / canola / olive oil
