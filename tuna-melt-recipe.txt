1 can tuna
1 oz shredded cheddar cheese
2 slices bread
2 green onions
3 tablespoons mayo

1. mix mayo and tuna and chopped green onions in a bowl.
2. put shredded cheese on bread slices
3. place tuna and mayo mixture between two slices of bread and cheese.
4. Toast the bread in a cast iron frying pan as though the sandwich were a regular grill cheese sandwich.
5. eat delicious tuna sandwich. yummy yummy yummy
