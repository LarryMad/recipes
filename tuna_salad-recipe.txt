Ingredients:
- 1 (7 ounce) can white tuna, drained and flaked
- 6 tablespoons mayonnaise or salad dressing
- 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese
- 3 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
- 1/8 teaspoon dried minced onion flakes
- 1/4 teaspoon curry powder
- 1 tablespoon dried parsley
- 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
- 1 pinch garlic powder

Directions:

1. In a medium bowl, stir together the tuna, mayonnaise, Parmesan
cheese, and onion flakes. Season with curry powder, parsley, dill and
garlic powder. Mix well and serve with crackers or on a sandwich.
