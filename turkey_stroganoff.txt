Ingredients:
    -1lb (Your choice of meat)
    -8oz sour cream
    -1 pack of mcCormic stroganoff seasoning
    -1C water
    -2 cups cooked rice

Directions:
    1. brown meat in pan untill it is no longer raw
    2. add water let it boil cover and simmer for 3 minutes
    3. add sour cream and stir. simmer to desired thickness of sauce
    4. serve over rice
