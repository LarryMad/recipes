Ingredients  for Upma:
1. Rava (100g)
2. Tomato (1)
3. Onion (1 medium)
4. Curry Leaves.
5. Ghee or oil.
6. Salt


Steps to make Upma:
1. Heat Ghee in a frying pan.
2. Put Rava in the pan and fry until it turns brown.
3. Then heat oil/ghee in pan and fry Onion until the turn brown
4. Add Tomato and curry leave 
5. Put roasted rava in the pan.
6. Add Salt to taste.
7. Add 2 cups of water.
8. Heat and mix until water dries out.
