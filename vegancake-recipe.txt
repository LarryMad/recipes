Ingredients

- 2 sweet potatoes

- cocoa

- cinnamon

- chocolate tablet (sugar free)

- cocoa butter

- 4 eggs

- 3 / 4 bananas

Steps

1. take out the skin of all ingredients

2. Use a food processor for the sweet potatoes and the bananas

3. Mix the processed sweet potatoes and bananas in a bowl and
add the eggs.
4. Add 3 spoons of cinnamon and cocoa to the mix.

5. Melt the chocolate tablet along with the cocoa butter

6. Mix everything

7. pre-heat the oven with 5 minutes.

8. Add the mix in a cake recipient and heat over 30 minutes
