Ingredients:
 - 500g mushrooms
 - 1 broccoli
 - 300g chicken filet
 - black pepper
 - garlic powder

Directions:
 1. Brown the chicken in a pan with some olive oil
 2. Cut the broccoli in small parts and add them in the pan.
 3. Cut the mushrooms in parts and add them in the pan.
 4. Add garlic powder and pepper to your taste.
 5. Allow to cook slowly on small fire for 20 minutes.
