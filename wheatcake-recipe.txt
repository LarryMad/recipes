Ingredients:
2 cups wheat flour
2 cups water
1 egg
salt to taste

Directions:
1.Add the ingredients in a bowl and make a batter.

2.Heat the pan on medium flame on the stove.

3.Pour the batter on the pan and let it cook for 2 minutes.

4.Flip the wheatcake and let it cook for another 2 minutes and remove it from the pan.
