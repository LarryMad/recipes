Ingredients:
 - 2 zucchinis
 - 7 cloves garlic
 - One 8-ounce can tomato sauce
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon red pepper
 - 1 tablespoon olive oil
 - 1/2 cup water

Directions:
 1. Cut the zucchinis into small cylinder-like pieces
 2. Add zucchinis into a pan with garlic, tomato sauce, cumin and salt
 3. Add red pepper, olive oil and water
 4. Cook it for half an hour while the lid is closed.
